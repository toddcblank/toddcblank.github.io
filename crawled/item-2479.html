Shield of Speed and EvasionLevel 4 Uncommon

A serious strike that slides past this shield might fortify your ability to return the favor.

Arms Slot: Any shield        840 gp

Power Daily (Immediate Reaction)

Use this power when an attacker scores a critical hit against your AC or Reflex defense. Regain the use of an encounter power, or spend an action point to regain the use of a daily power. The power regained must be of a level equal to or lower than the level of this item.
