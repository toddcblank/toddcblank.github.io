Venomscorched Eidolon
Large natural animate (construct)
Level 13 Elite Controller XP 1600

Initiative +8        Senses Perception +9
Fearless Followers aura 5; allies in the aura are immune to fear.
HP 264; Bloodied 132
AC 30; Fortitude 28, Reflex 22, Will 23
Immune disease, fear, sleep
Saving Throws +2
Speed 5
Action Points 1

Slam (standard, at-will)

Reach 2; +19 vs AC; 2d8+6 damage.

Toxic Assault (standard, at-will) Poison

The eidolon makes two slam attacks. If both slam attacks hit the same target, the eidolon makes a secondary attack. Secondary Attack: Close burst 1; +17 vs Fortitude; 1d8+3 poison damage and the target is slowed (save ends).

Toxic Rebuke (immediate reaction, when an enemy attacks the eidolon while unhallowed stance is active, at-will)

Ranged 20; +17 vs Reflex; 2d8+5 poison damage. Miss: Half damage. This attack does not provoke opportunity attacks.

Storm of Death (immediate reaction, when an enemy kills one of the eidolon’s allies in the eidolon’s line of sight, at-will) Necrotic

Ranged 20; +17 vs Reflex; 1d6+5 necrotic damage, and the target is blinded and takes ongoing 5 necrotic damage (save ends both). This attack does not provoke opportunity attacks.

Unhallowed Stance (standard; at-will) Poison

The eidolon assumes a meditative stance. Until the end of its next turn, the eidolon gains resist 20 to all damage, and all allies in its line of sight deal an extra 1d8 poison damage on their melee attacks. If the eidolon moves, the effect ends.
