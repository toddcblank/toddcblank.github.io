Traits

Angelic Presence

While the angel is not bloodied, creatures take a -2 penalty to attack rolls against it.

Threatening Reach

The angel can make opportunity attacks against enemies within 3 squares of it.

Standard Actions

Spear (thunder, weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 3 (one creature); +29 vs. AC

Hit: 1d10 + 5 damage plus 2d10 thunder damage.

Light of Justice (radiant) Encounter

Attack: Ranged 10 (one creature); +27 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 3d10 + 20 radiant damage.

Effect: The angel marks the target until the end of the encounter.

Astral Brilliance (radiant, zone) Encounter

Attack: Close burst 3 (enemies in the burst); +27 vs. Reflex

Hit: 4d10 + 10 radiant damage.

Effect: The burst creates a zone that lasts until the end of the encounter. Whenever the angel uses spear while in the zone, its attack deals 1d10 extra radiant damage.
