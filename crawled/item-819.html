Bronzewood Coils TotemLevel 13+ Uncommon

With this knotted serpent totem crafted of bronzewood, you can grant your spirit companion an aspect of the World Serpent.

Lvl 13+317,000 gp Lvl 23+5425,000 gp
Lvl 18+485,000 gp Lvl 28+62,125,000 gp

Implement: Totem

Enhancement Bonus: attack rolls and damage rolls

Critical: +1d6 damage per plus

Power Daily (Free Action)

Trigger: You hit an enemy with the spirit's fangs power (Player's Handbook 2, page 120) using this totem.

Effect: That enemy is grabbed by your spirit companion. When the enemy attempts to escape the grab, you can have it make the Acrobatics check or Athletics check against your Will instead of your Fortitude or Reflex.
