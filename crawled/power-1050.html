Rogue Utility 16Hide in Plain Sight

You stand unseen in the midst of the battle, striking from your place of hiding.

Encounter        Martial
Minor Action      Personal

Prerequisite: You must be trained in Stealth.

Requirement: You must be hidden.

Effect: You are invisible until you leave your current space or until the end of the encounter. No other action that you perform makes you visible.
