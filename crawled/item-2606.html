Staff of Fiery MightLevel 2+ Common

This staff is engraved with fire symbols and is warm to the touch. It makes fire spells more potent.

Lvl 2+1520 gp Lvl 17+465,000 gp
Lvl 7+22,600 gp Lvl 22+5325,000 gp
Lvl 12+313,000 gp Lvl 27+61,625,000 gp

Implement: Staff

Enhancement Bonus: attack rolls and damage rolls

Critical: +1d8 fire damage per plus

Power Daily (Free Action)

Use this power when using a power that has the fire keyword. After rolling damage, you can reroll a number of damage dice equal to or less than the staff's enhancement bonus. You must keep the new results, and you can't reroll any die more than once.
