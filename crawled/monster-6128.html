Traits

Nine Lives

Shelsa initially has eight extra lives. While Shelsa has five extra lives remaining, she gains a +2 bonus to defenses, attack rolls, and damage. While she has two extra lives remaining, this bonus increases to +4.

Standard Actions

Bite At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +17 vs. AC

Hit: 10 damage.

Mobile Attack At-Will

Effect: Shelsa shifts up to her speed and uses bite once during the movement.

Move Actions

Guardian Jump (teleportation) Encounter

Effect: Shelsa teleports up to 10 squares.

Triggered Actions

Limited Rebirth (healing, teleportation) Recharge when Shelsa spends one of her extra lives

Trigger: Shelsa drops to 0 hit points while she has an extra life remaining.

Effect (No Action): Shelsa spends one of her extra lives and regains 1 hit point. She can then teleport up to 10 squares, and she becomes invisible until the end of her next turn or until she attacks.
