Bardic Wayfarer

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Bard, majestic word class feature
Benefit: Once per turn when you teleport an ally with a power, you can also teleport 1 square as a free action.
    In addition, when you use majestic word you can teleport the target 1 square instead of sliding the target.

Published in Dragon Magazine 387, page(s) 25.
