Ray of Feywild SunshineLevel 17 Common

These rays restore dead or withered foliage, renewing desolate lands with sudden summer growth and causing fruit to grow and ripen in the dead of winter.

Consumable        2,600 gp

Utility Power (Zone) Consumable (Minor Action)

Effect: You release the ray from its vessel, and it creates a zone of bright light in a close burst 20. The light functions as sunlight, and the zone lasts until the end of the encounter. In addition, plants in the zone are restored to peak life.



Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 139.
