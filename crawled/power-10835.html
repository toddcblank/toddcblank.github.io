Daring Slinger Attack 20Dispersing Shot

You let fly a forceful shot that shatters armor, flesh, and bone, knocking your enemy senseless.

Daily        Martial, Reliable, Weapon
Standard Action      Ranged weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a sling.

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. AC

Hit: 4[W] + Dexterity modifier damage, and the target is dazed and slowed (save ends both). If you have combat advantage against the target, it is immobilized instead of slowed.

  Aftereffect: The target grants combat advantage (save ends).


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 74.
