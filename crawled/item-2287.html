Robe of the ArchfiendLevel 20+ Uncommon

Woven from the skin of humans, devils, and demons, these grim robes draw a viewer's eyes to the dominating gaze of the wearer.

Armor: Cloth

Enhancement Bonus: AC

Power Daily (Immediate Reaction)

Use this power when a creature within 10 squares of you attacks you. Make an attack: Charisma vs. Will (add the robe's enhancement bonus as an enhancement bonus to the attack roll); on a hit, the target is dominated until the end of your next turn.

Sustain Minor: Repeat the attack. On a hit, the target remains dominated. If the attack fails, you can no longer sustain this power.
