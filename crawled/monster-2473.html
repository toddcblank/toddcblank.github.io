Divine Inspiration (minor, encounter)

Close burst 10; all allies in the burst gains a +10 bonus to damage rolls and deal a critical hit on a roll of 18–20, until the end of the Imperator’s next turn. (This bonus damage does not stack with inspiring leader.)

Vicious Harrier (encounter)

If the Imperator and an ally are both adjacent to the same enemy, that enemy grants combat advantage to them even if they are not flanking.
