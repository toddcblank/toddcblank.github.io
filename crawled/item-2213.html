Ring of FearlessnessLevel 28 Uncommon

This thin ivory band wards your mind from fear.

Ring Slot        2,125,000 gp

Property

You gain a +4 bonus to all defenses against fear attacks.

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

Trigger: An enemy within your line of sight makes a fear attack.

Effect: Each ally within 5 squares of you gains a +4 power bonus to all defenses against that attack.
    If you've reached at least one milestone today, each ally within 10 squares of you gains the bonus instead.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 71.
