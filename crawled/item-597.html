Bane's CollarLevel 28 Uncommon

Your mind shrugs off the control of others while you wear this bright steel collar, but an ally pays the price.

Lvl 28+62,125,000 gp

Neck Slot

Enhancement Bonus: Fortitude, Reflex, and Will

Property

When you make a saving throw against a charm or fear effect, you can roll twice and use either result.

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

Trigger: An enemy targets you with an charm or fear attack.

Effect: An ally within 10 squares of you becomes the target of the attack.
