Sorcerer Utility 22Storm Body

The spirit of the storm engulfs you, and you become as the wind and lightning, heedless of the earth and dangerous to the touch.

Encounter        Arcane, Lightning
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You become insubstantial until the start of your next turn and can fly 10 squares with this move action. Each creature that makes a melee attack against you during this move takes lightning damage equal to your Charisma modifier.


Published in Arcane Power, page(s) 38.
