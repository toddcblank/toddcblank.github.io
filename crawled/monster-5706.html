Attack: Ranged 15 (one creature); +20 vs. Reflex

Hit: 1d10 + 13 necrotic damage, and ongoing 5 cold and necrotic damage (save ends).

Night Tendrils (force, necrotic, zone) Encounter

Attack: Area burst 2 within 15 (enemies in the burst); +20 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 1d10 + 11 damage, and ongoing 5 force and necrotic damage (save ends).

Effect: The burst creates a zone that lasts until the end of the encounter. Enemies have vulnerable 5 necrotic while in the zone. Allies treat radiant damage they take as necrotic damage while in the zone. As a minor action, the conjurer can move the zone up to 2 squares.
