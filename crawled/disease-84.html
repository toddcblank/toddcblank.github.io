Level 31 DiseaseWasting Plague

The Lord of Misery and Despair waits to feed on the souls of his victims.

Stage 0: The target is cured.
Stage 1: Initial Effect: The target is weakened.
Stage 2: The target takes a -2 penalty to Fortitude and dies after two failed death saving throws instead of three. The target also takes ongoing 25 damage (save ends).
    The target takes an additional -2 penalty to Fortitude and dies when it drops to 0 hit points or fewer. The target also takes ongoing 25 damage (save ends).
Stage 3: Final State: The target dies and its soul feeds Phraxas. If Phraxas has been destroyed, he is reborn in the flesh of the target.
Check: At the end of each extended rest, the target makes a check if it is at stage 1 or 2.
35 or lower: The stage of the disease increases by one.
36-40: No Change
41 or higher: The stage of the disease decreases by one.

Published in Demonomicon, page(s) 135.
