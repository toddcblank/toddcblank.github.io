Turaglas
Gargantuan elemental magical beast (blind, demon, ooze)
Level 30 Solo Brute XP 155000

Initiative +18        Senses ; blindsight 100, tremorsense 20
Infinite Maws aura 0; at the start of Turaglas’s turn, each creature in Turaglas’s space takes 10 damage and 10 acid damage and Turaglas slides it 1 square.
HP 1128; Bloodied 564
AC 44; Fortitude 44, Reflex 40, Will 43
Immune disease, gaze; Resist 25 acid, 10 variable (3/encounter)
Saving Throws +5
Speed 4, climb 4 (spider climb)
Action Points 2

Bite (standard, at-will) Acid

+35 vs AC; 2d6+4 damage plus 2d6 acid damage, and the target is slowed (save ends). Reach 0

Caustic Spittle (standard, at-will) Acid, Necrotic

Ranged 20; one or two targets; +33 vs Reflex; 2d12+8 acid damage, and the target is slowed (save ends).

Grasping Mouths (minor, at-will) Acid

+35 vs AC; 2d6+8 damage, plus 2d6 acid damage, and the target is pulled into Turaglas’s space and slowed (save ends). Reach 3

Forbidding Mouths (immediate interrupt, when an enemy leaves turaglas' space, at-will)

Reach 3; +35 vs AC; the target is pulled into Turaglas' space. targets the triggering creature

Tide of Flesh (standard, recharge ) Acid

Close burst 3; +33 vs Reflex; Turaglas washes outward in a wave of flesh and then retracts; 2d12 + 15 acid damage, and the target is pulled into Turaglas’s space and slowed (save ends).

Caustic Spray (standard, at-will) Acid

Close blast 6; +31 vs Reflex; 2d10+8 acid damage, plus the target is slowed (save ends).

Expand (standard, recharge

Close burst 1; targets creatures outside Turaglas' space; +33 vs Reflex; 2d6+8 damage and 2d6 acid damage.
Miss: The target can shift 1 square as a free action to avoid Turaglas as it expands. Effect: Turaglas grows to fill the burst. At the end of the encounter, Turaglas returns to its standard size (typically 5 squares by 5 squares).

Contract (minor; recharges when turaglas uses expand) Acid

Turaglas shrinks in space by 1 square. Each creature in Turaglas’ space is pulled 2 squares toward Turaglas’ center. Turaglas cannot become smaller than 5 squares by 5 squares.

Bloodied Expansion (standard, when first bloodied)

Turaglas recharges expand and uses it twice. Until the end of the encounter, expand recharges on a 5 or a 6.

Sea of Flesh

Turaglas consists of gooey flesh approximately waist high in the squares it occupies. It can enter and occupy squares occupied by other creatures, and other creatures can enter and occupy squares occupied by Turaglas. Turaglas’s space is difficult terrain to all creatures except Turaglas.

Uncontainable Consciousness

When Turaglas is dazed, it instead grants combat advantage to all enemies for the same duration. When Turaglas is stunned, it grants combat advantage to all enemies and is immobilized for the same duration.

Digested Essence (standard, when a living enemy drops to 0 hit points or fewer while in turaglas' space)

Turaglas regains hit points equal to the triggering creature’s bloodied value or gains 1 action point.

Dimensional Step (minor; recharge )

Turaglas teleports up to 12 squares.
