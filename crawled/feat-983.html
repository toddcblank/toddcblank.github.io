Menacing Brute Style [Arena Fighting]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Con 13 or Cha 13, any martial class
Benefit: You gain a benefit with any of the following exploits you possess.


Associated Powers:
Brash Assault: If the target makes a melee basic attack against you, it grants combat advantage to one ally within 5 squares until the end of your next turn.
Brash Strike: If you hit with this exploit, the target also takes a -1 penalty to Will defense until the end of your next turn.
Circling Strike: If you hit with this exploit and the target moves or shifts before the end of your next turn, your beast companion can shift 1 square as a free action.

Published in Dragon Magazine Annual, Dragon Magazine 368, page(s) 61.
