Standard Actions

Life-Draining Touch (fear, necrotic, psychic) At-Will

Attack: Melee 2 (one creature); +29 vs. Reflex

Hit: 3d8 + 21 necrotic and psychic damage.

Attack: Close burst 3 (enemies in the burst); +29 vs. Fortitude

    Second Failed Saving Throw: The target drops to 0 hit points. If it is already at 0 hit points or fewer, it dies.

Shadow Stalk (teleportation) Recharge when first bloodied

Effect: The atropal teleports up to 10 squares.

Attack: Ranged 10 (one creature); +29 vs. Will (This attack does not provoke opportunity attacks.)

Hit: The target loses all resistances and immunities, and when making saving throws, it must roll twice and use the lower result (save ends all).
