Level 6 Striker-

HP 53; Bloodied 26; Healing Surges 9; Surge Value 13Initiative +5
AC 21, Fortitude 20, Reflex 18, Will 19Perception+6
Speed 5Darkvision

Traits

Combat Advantage

Thresher deals 1d6 extra damage against any target granting combat advantage to her.

Troglodyte Stench Aura 1

Living enemies take a -2 penalty to attack rolls while in the aura.

Standard Actions

Claw At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +12 vs. AC

Hit: 1d6 + 7 damage.

Tooth and Claw Encounter

Attack: Melee 1 (one or two creatures); +12 vs. AC. If Thresher targets only one creature, she can make this attack twice against that creature.

Hit: 1d6 + 7 damage. If the attack bloodies the target, Thresher can use claw against it as a free action.

Feral Pounce Encounter

Effect: Thresher shifts up to 2 squares before the attack.

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +12 vs. AC

Hit: 3d6 + 7 damage, and Thresher shifts up to 2 squares. If Thresher has cover or concealment after shifting, she can make a Stealth check to hide as a free action.

Minor Actions

Blind Spot Advantage Encounter

Effect: Thresher gains concealment against one enemy she can see until the end of her next turn.
