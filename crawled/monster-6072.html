Geisha Bushi
Medium natural humanoid, human
Level 7 Soldier XP 300

HP 77; Bloodied 38Initiative +9
AC 23, Fortitude 18, Reflex 21, Will 18Perception+10
Speed 6

Standard Actions

Wakizashi (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +12 vs. AC

Hit: 2d8 + 6 damage.

Effect: The target is marked until the end of the bushi’s next turn.

Shuriken to the Gut (weapon) Encounter

Attack: Ranged 5 (one creature); +12 vs. AC. This attack doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks.

Hit: 3d6 + 5 damage, and the target is immobilized and takes ongoing 5 damage (save ends both).

Miss: The target is immobilized until the end of its next turn.

Triggered Actions

Stand, You Coward At-Will

Trigger: An enemy within 5 squares of the bushi and marked by it uses an attack power that doesn’t include it as a target.

Effect (Immediate Reaction): Shuriken to the gut recharges, and the bushi uses it against the triggering enemy.
