Water Dervish
Small elemental magical beast (air, earth)
Level 3 Skirmisher XP 150

Initiative +7        Senses Perception +1
HP 46; Bloodied 23
AC 17; Fortitude 14, Reflex 16, Will 14
Immune disease, poison
Speed 8

Waterspout (standard, at-will)

Melee 1; +6 vs Reflex; 2d6 + 4 damage.

Fury of the Storm (move, recharge )

The water dervish shifts 5 squares and attacks each enemy it moves adjacent to during the movement: +8 vs. Fortitude, and the dervish slides the target 2 squares and knocks it prone. A creature in the water when it is knocked prone takes 10 damage instead.
