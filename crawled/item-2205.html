Ring of Circling FangsLevel 20 Uncommon

This slim, silver ring is engraved with a simple circle.

Ring Slot        125,000 gp

Properties

  • While you are bloodied, you gain an aura of force daggers. Any adjacent creature that attacks you takes 10 force damage.
  • As a minor action, you can cause the daggers to shed dim light to a radius of 5 squares. The light can be extinguished with a free action.

Power (Force) Encounter (Standard Action)

Effect: One creature within 3 squares of you takes 20 force damage.



Published in Dungeon Magazine 181.
