Face-Stealing RingLevel 18 Uncommon

This wearer of this ivory ring sees the faces of others as potential disguises.

Ring Slot        85,000 gp

Property

You gain a +2 item bonus to Insight checks.

Power (Illusion) Daily (Standard Action)

You assume the exact appearance of an adjacent humanoid creature. You also gain the creature's mannerisms, voice, and speech patterns. This effect lasts for 1 hour or until you dismiss it (a free action). You gain a +5 power bonus to Bluff checks to pass yourself off as the creature you're imitating. If you've reached at least one milestone today, the effect lasts for one day and you can use a standard action to recall the effect after dismissing it.
