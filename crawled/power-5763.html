Swordmage Attack 17Snake Strike

Your sword extends into the form of a shadowy asp and bites an enemy. If that foe dares to leave your side, poison assails it a second time.

Encounter        Arcane, Implement, Poison
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Intelligence vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d8 + Intelligence modifier poison damage. If the target ends its next turn in a space that is not adjacent to you, it takes 1d8 + your Constitution modifier poison damage.


Published in Arcane Power, page(s) 59.
