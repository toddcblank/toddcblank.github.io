Warlord Attack 9Awakened Wrath

Seeing your hurts, an ally explodes with unexpected violence.

Daily        Martial
Immediate Reaction      Close burst 5

Trigger: You are bloodied by an attack

Target: One ally with line of sight to you

Effect: The target makes a melee basic attack against each enemy adjacent to him or her as a free action. Then the target can shift 1 square for each enemy he or she hit as a free action, but the target must end this movement closer to you.


Published in Dragon Magazine 381, page(s) 33.
