Grusildrith, Bog Hag
Medium fey humanoid (aquatic)
Level 10 Elite Skirmisher XP 1000

Initiative +11        Senses Perception +7; low-light vision
HP 206; Bloodied 103
AC 24; Fortitude 23, Reflex 22, Will 21
Saving Throws +2
Speed 8 (swamp walk), swim 8
Action Points 1

Claw (standard, at-will)

+15 vs AC; 1d8+6 damage.

Hag's Curse (minor, at-will)

Targets the nearest enemy to Grusildrith that she can see; the target is cursed until the end of the encounter or until it drops to 0 hit points or fewer. Grusildrith deals 1d6 extra
damage to a creature cursed by her

Rending Claws (standard, at-will)

Grusildith makes two claw attacks against the same target; if both claws hit, she deals 5 extra damage to the target.

Hag's Hex (standard, at-will) Psychic

Ranged 10; +12 vs Reflex; 1d10+3 psychic damage.

Change Shape (minor; at-will) Polymorph

A bog hag can alter its physical form to appear as a beautiful young female elf, half-elf, eladrin, or human (see Change Shape, Monster Manual, page 280).

Evasive Charge

Grusildith shifts 2 squares after charging.

Misty Step

When an enemy cursed by Grusildith drops to 0 hit points or fewer, she can teleport 5 squares as a free action.

Skills Intimidate +13, Nature +12, Stealth +14
