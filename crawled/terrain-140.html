At-Will TerrainTree of Life Defiling

The tree of life pulses with vitality, fueling the powers of a greedy defiler.

Free Action

Requirement: You must be within 5 squares of a tree of life.

Effect: When you use arcane defiling or a necrotic power and deal damage to one or more of your allies, the tree of life takes that damage instead.

Published in Dark Sun Creature Catalog, page(s) 137.
