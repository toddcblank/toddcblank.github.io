Invoker Attack 5Icon of Terror

You invoke the Icon of Terror, an image that was once used to drive the beings of the Far Realm from this reality. You drive nearby creatures back in fear as the icon assaults their minds.

Daily        Divine, Fear, Implement, Psychic
Standard Action      Close blast 5

Target: Each creature in the blast

Attack: Wisdom vs. Will

Hit: 2d6 + Wisdom modifier psychic damage, and you push the target 2 squares.

  Covenant of Wrath: The number of squares you push the target equals your Constitution modifier.

Miss: Half damage, and you push the target 1 square.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 106.
