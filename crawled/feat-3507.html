Delzoun True Born

Prerequisite: Dwarf
Benefit: Whenever you use stand your ground to make a saving throw to avoid falling prone, you can make the saving throw twice and use the more beneficial of the two results.
Special: You are considered to be a member of the lost kingdom of Delzoun for the purposes of all magic effects, items, and treaties that depend upon that distinction.

Published in Dungeon Magazine 183.
