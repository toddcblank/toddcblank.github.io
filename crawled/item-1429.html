Gloves of Dimensional GraspLevel 16 Uncommon

With these skin-tight gloves you can create a small portal through which to manipulate objects.

Hands Slot        45,000 gp

Property

You gain a +4 item bonus to Thievery checks.

Power Daily (Standard Action)

You make a Thievery check against a target up to 5 squares away from you. You must have line of sight to the target.
