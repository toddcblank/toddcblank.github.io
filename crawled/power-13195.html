Monk Utility 16Pearl of Black Doubt

As the enemy’s attack goes wide, you plant doubt’s bitter seed in its mind to hamper its fight against you.

Daily        Psionic, Stance
Immediate Reaction      Personal

Trigger: An adjacent enemy misses you with an attack

Effect: Until the stance ends, you gain a +2 power bonus to all defenses against the triggering enemy’s attacks. In addition, whenever the triggering enemy misses you with an attack, you gain a +2 power bonus to attack rolls for any attack that includes the triggering enemy made before the end of your next turn.


Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 68.
