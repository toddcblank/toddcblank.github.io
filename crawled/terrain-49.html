Phase Crystal

This translucent, silvery crystal is related to phase rock (see Manual of the Planes). It can be induced to discharge its extradimensional energy and shunt a creature out of phase for a short time.

    Effect: When a creature ends its move in a square of phase crystal, it gains phasing until the end of its next turn. That square cannot affect another creature until the end of the encounter.
    Usage: Combined with obstacles that restrict a battlefield, phase crystal allows characters or monsters greater mobility.
