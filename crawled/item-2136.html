Rat Form ArmorLevel 8+ Uncommon

Tiny, sleek skins seem to make up this coat of leather, which has tassels that look disturbingly like rodent tails. With it, you can take the stealthy shape of a rat.

Armor: Leather

Enhancement Bonus: AC

Power (Polymorph) Daily (Standard Action)

You and your gear assume the form of a common sewer rat. While in this form:

*: You can't attack.

*: Your gear is merged into your form and unusable.

*: You gain a +5 bonus to Stealth checks.

*: All your defenses remain the same.

You can sustain this power as a standard action on your turn and end the power to return to your normal form as a free action. You resume normal form if knocked unconscious or dropped to 0 or fewer hit points.
