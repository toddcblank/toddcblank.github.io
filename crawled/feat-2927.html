Rose King's Run

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Avenger or swordmage, proficiency with longsword
Benefit: You gain a +2 feat bonus to Athletics checks.
When you hit an enemy with an attack power associated with this feat while using a longsword, you gain a climb speed equal to your speed -2 until the end of your turn.


Associated Powers: Bond of Retribution, Luring Strike

Published in Dragon Magazine 386, page(s) 66.
