Carcanet of Psychic SchismLevel 15 Uncommon

This ornate headband protects your mind by splitting it in two, but there's a price to pay.

Head Slot        25,000 gp

Property

Gain a +1 bonus to Will defense.

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

Use this power when an attack would make you stunned, dazed, or dominated. You are unaffected by that condition, and you instead take a -2 penalty to attack rolls and Will defense for the condition's normal duration.
