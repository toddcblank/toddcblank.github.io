Lord Padraig can make opportunity attacks against enemies within 2 squares of him.

Standard Actions

Halberd (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 2 (one creature); +17 vs. AC

Hit: 3d10 + 7 damage, and each ally adjacent to the target can make a melee basic attack against the target as an opportunity action.

Call to Arms Encounter

Trigger: Lord Padraig is first bloodied.

Effect (Free Action): Close burst 10 (allies in the burst); the target makes a melee basic attack or shifts up to 3 squares as a free action.

Bound to the Deck 3/Encounter

Trigger: Lord Padraig rolls initiative or his hit point total first drops to 139 or 69.

Effect (No Action): Lord Padraig draws a new card from among the cards in his possession and uses the power associated with that card.
