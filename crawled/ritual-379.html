Reduce Beast

Component Cost: 75 gp and 1 healing surge
Market Price: 520 gp
Key Skill: Arcana or Nature (no check)Level: 7
Category: Exploration
Time: 1 minute
Duration: 6 hours

One friendly Large or smaller beast present during the ritual’s performance shrinks and reduces in weight so that you can carry it in a pocket, a hand, or a pouch. The beast’s statistics are unchanged, but it cannot attack while shrunken.
    You can use a minor action to end the ritual’s effects. Once the ritual ends, the beast teleports to the nearest unoccupied space big enough to accommodate its normal size. Each round after the ritual ends, the beast grows one size category, starting at Tiny, until it returns to its normal size.

Published in Dragon Magazine 405.
