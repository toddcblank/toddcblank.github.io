Traits

Pack Attack

The claw fighter’s attacks deal 5 extra damage to any enemy that has two or more of the claw fighter’s allies adjacent to it.

Standard Actions

Claw At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +11 vs. AC

Hit: 2d6 + 7 damage, or 2d6 + 9 while bloodied.

Clawing Charge At-Will

Effect: When it charges, the claw fighter uses claw twice against a single target.

Mobile Melee Attack At-Will

Effect: The claw fighter can move up to 4 squares and use claw at any point during that movement. The claw fighter does not provoke opportunity attacks when moving away from the target of its attack.
