Barbarian Attack 15Iron Hammer Rage

Even the iron that sleeps in the earth has its primal spirits, which fuel your mighty attacks to dash your foes against walls and large trees.

Daily        Primal, Rage, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage, and you push the target a number of squares equal to your Strength modifier.

Miss: Half damage, and you push the target 1 square.

Effect: You enter the rage of the iron hammer. Until the rage ends, whenever you hit a creature with a melee attack, you push that creature 2 squares. If the creature is adjacent to blocking terrain at the end of the push, the creature takes damage equal to your Strength modifier.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 57.
