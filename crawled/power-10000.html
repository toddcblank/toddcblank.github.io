Fighter Utility 10Mighty Surge

You dig deep to find the strength you need to overcome your foes.

Daily        Healing, Martial
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You can spend a healing surge, and you also gain a +2 power bonus to all defenses and a +1 power bonus to attack rolls until the end of your next turn. Until the end of the encounter, each time you spend a healing surge, you gain a +2 power bonus to all defenses and a +1 power bonus to attack rolls until the end of your next turn.


Published in Dragon Magazine 379, page(s) 64.
