Fortify Beast

Your technique ensures that your beast will carry you through difficult situations.

Component Cost: 1 healing surge
Market Price: 250 gp
Key Skill: Nature (no check)Level: 5
Category: Martial Practice
Time: 10 minutes
Duration: Special

You prepare up to six natural beasts that have the mount keyword for the hardships ahead. Each beast gains temporary hit points equal to your healing surge value. These temporary hit points last until you take an extended rest.

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 149.
