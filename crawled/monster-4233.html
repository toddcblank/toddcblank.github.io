Storm of Strikes (standard, encounter)

The ritual-born killer shifts up to 4 squares, makes one slam against all adjacent targets (without shifting), and then shifts 1 square for each attack it made.

The ritual-born killer shifts up to 4 squares and makes up to two slam attacks.
