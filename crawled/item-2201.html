Ring of Arcane InformationLevel 17 Uncommon

This ring helps you discern the nature of arcane phenomena.

Ring Slot        65,000 gp

Property

You gain a +5 item bonus to Arcana checks made to detect magic.

Power Daily (Minor Action)

You detect magic within 20 squares of you in every direction, and you can ignore any sources of magical energy you're already aware of. Ignore all barriers; you can detect magic through walls, doors, and such.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, you also learn the name, power source, and keywords of any magical effects in the area.



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 157.
