Ragnorra moves its speed and can move through enemies’ spaces. When it enters and enemy’s space Ragnorra makes the following attack: +33 vs. Reflex; 2d10 + 9 and the target is knocked prone. Ragnorra must end this move in an unoccupied space, but can make a melee basic attack at the end of this move.

Close burst 10; targets up to 4 enemies in the burst; +33 vs Fortitude; 1d12 + 9 and ongoing 15 damage (save ends). Aftereffect: the target is dazed and takes ongoing 5 damage (save ends both).

Reflect Power (immediate reaction, when missed by a power that attacks against fortitude, reflex, or will; recharge )

Ragnorra reflects a portion of the power back onto the character who attacked her; that character takes 20 damage of the same type as the attack.
