+13 vs AC; 2d6+5 damage.

Winding Strike (standard, recharge )

+13 vs AC; 2d6+5 damage, and the target slides 2 squares and is grabbed and restrained.

Guarded Area

When making attacks against a creature in its guarded area, the writhing chain gains a +2 bonus to hit, and can stretch itself, granting itself reach 3 on all melee attacks.

Threatening Reach (with guarded area only)

This creature can make opportunity attacks against all enemies within its reach (3 squares).

Serpentine Strike (move; at-will)

The chain shifts 3 squares.
