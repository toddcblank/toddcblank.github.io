Troll Runt
Medium natural humanoid
Level 12 Minion Brute XP 175

Initiative +9        Senses Perception +8
HP 1; a missed attack never damages a minion.
AC 24; Fortitude 25, Reflex 24, Will 22
Speed 8

Claw (standard, at-will)

+15 vs AC; 10 damage.

Troll Runt Healing Healing

If the troll runt is reduced to 0 hit points by an attack that does not deal acid or fire damage, it falls prone and remains at 0 hit points until the start of its next turn, when it regains 1 hit point. If an attack deals acid or fire damage to the troll runt while it is at 0 hit points, it is destroyed.
