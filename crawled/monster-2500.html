Urishtar, Shadow Dragon
Huge shadow magical beast (dragon)
Level 20 Solo Lurker XP 14000

Initiative +24        Senses Perception +20; Darkvision
HP 730; Bloodied 365
AC 36; Fortitude 34, Reflex 36, Will 32
Resist 30 necrotic; Vulnerable radiant (whenever a shadow dragon takes radiant damage, one of its globes of darkness ends)
Saving Throws +5
Speed 10, fly 10 (hover), overland flight 20, swim 10
Action Points 2

Bite (standard, at-will) Necrotic

Reach 3; +25 vs AC; 2d10+5 damage, and the target is weakened (save ends).

Claw (standard, at-will)

Reach 3; +25 vs AC; 1d10+5 damage.

Draconic Frenzy (standard, at-will)

The dragon makes a bite attack and a claw attack.

Tail Slash (immediate reaction, when an enemy misses the dragon with a melee attack, at-will)

the dragon attacks the target; targets the enemy that missed it; +25 vs AC; 2d8+5 damage.

Breath Weapon (standard, recharge ) Necrotic

Close blast 5; +23 vs Fortitude; 2d10+5 necrotic damage, and the target loses one healing surge and is weakened (save ends). Aftereffect: The target's necrotic damage is negated until the end of the encounter. Miss: Half damage, and the target does not lose a healing surge.

Bloodied Breath (free, encounter) Necrotic

The dragon’s breath weapon recharges, and the dragon uses it immediately.

Frightful Presence (standard, encounter) Fear

Close burst 5; targets enemies; +23 vs Will; the target is dazed until the end of the dragon's next turn. Aftereffect: The target takes a -2 penalty to attack rolls (save ends).

Globe of Darkness (minor 1/round; sustain minor, at-will) Zone

Area burst 2 within 10; this power creates a zone of darkness that remains in place until the end of the dragon’s next turn. The zone blocks line of sight for all creatures except the dragon. Any creature entirely within the area (except the dragon) is blinded.

Combat Advantage

The shadow dragon deals an extra 4d6 damage against a target it has combat advantage against.

Bloodied Rage

The dragon deals an extra 2d6 damage with all attacks while it is bloodied.

Shadow Walk (move; at-will) Teleportation

A shadow dragon that is in at least one square of a globe of darkness can teleport to any other globe of darkness within line of sight. It must end this move in at least one square of that globe of darkness.

Alignment Evil        Languages Common, Draconic
Skills Intimidate +18, Stealth +24
Str 24 (+17)      Dex 28 (+19)      Wis 21 (+15)
Con 20 (+15)      Int 18 (+14)      Cha 17 (+13)
