Druid Attack 17Rolling Earth

You strike the ground with your open palm, causing it to shift and roll like the waves of an ocean storm.

Encounter        Implement, Primal
Standard Action      Close blast 5

Target: Each creature in the blast

Attack: Wisdom vs. Reflex

Hit: 3d6 + Wisdom modifier damage, and you push the target up to 5 squares.

Effect: Choose up to 5 contiguous, unoccupied squares in the blast that are on a solid surface. Until the end of your next turn, those squares are blocking terrain that is up to 2 squares high.


Published in Heroes of the Feywild, page(s) 83.
