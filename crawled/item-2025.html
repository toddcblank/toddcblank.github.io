Pelor's ScepterLevel 29 Uncommon

This golden rod is capped with a sunburst that adds potency to your radiant powers.

Lvl 29+62,625,000 gp

Implement: Rod

Enhancement Bonus: attack rolls and damage rolls

Critical: +1d8 radiant damage per plus

Properties

  • Divine characters can use this rod as a holy symbol implement for divine powers.
  • When you hit an enemy with a divine radiant power using this rod, you can reroll any damage die that shows a 1. Against elemental creatures, you can reroll any damage die that shows a 1 or a 2.
