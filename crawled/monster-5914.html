Attack: Area burst 1 within 10 (enemies in the burst); +19 vs. Will

Hit: 3d12 + 5 psychic damage.

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, whenever Thakok-An hits the target with a fear effect, she can use a free action to slide the target up to 3 squares.
