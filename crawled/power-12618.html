Cleric Utility 10Battle Surge

You turn the tide of battle by healing allies near you and filling them with courage.

Daily        Divine, Healing
Standard Action      Close burst 5

Target: You and each ally in the burst

Effect: Each target regains hit points as if he or she had spent a healing surge. In addition, each target gains a +2 power bonus to attack rolls until the end of your next turn.


Published in Heroes of the Fallen Lands, page(s) 97.
