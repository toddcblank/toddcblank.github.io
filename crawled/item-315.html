Candle clocks (2)


Category: Gear
Price: 1 gp
Weight: 1 lb
Description: Lighting one of these wax tapers allows you to count hours by observing how far the candle has burned down against marks made in the wax. Each clock lasts for 8 hours and is consumed after use. Candle clocks are typically sold in pairs to help synchronize timing between separate groups.

Published in Mordenkainen's Magnificent Emporium, page(s) 124.
