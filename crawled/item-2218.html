Ring of FocusLevel 24 Uncommon

This ornate platinum and gold band is dotted with seven different gems.

Ring Slot        525,000 gp

Property

You gain a +1 item bonus to Will.

Power Daily (Minor Action)

Once per round until the end of the encounter, you can use a free action on your turn to sustain an effect of yours that requires a minor action to sustain.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, you can sustain a power once per round on your turn using no action, rather than a free action.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 71.
