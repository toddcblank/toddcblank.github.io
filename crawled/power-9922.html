Warden Attack 29Form of the Unruly Earth

A shell of stone and moss protects you from harm and lets you move freely over any terrain, even as earth spirits grasp at your chosen foes.

Daily        Polymorph, Primal
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You assume the guardian form of the unruly earth until the end of the encounter. While you are in this form, you gain a +2 bonus to Fortitude, and you ignore difficult terrain. Each enemy marked by you is slowed and takes a –4 penalty to AC until the mark ends.
Once during this encounter, you can use the Form of the Unruly Earth Attack power while you are in this form.


Form of the Unruly Earth Attack

Encounter      Polymorph, Primal, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 4[W] + Strength modifier damage. You knock the target prone, and it can’t stand up (save ends).

Miss: Half damage.

Requirement: The power Form of the Unruly Earth must be active in order to use this power.


Published in Primal Power, page(s) 106.
