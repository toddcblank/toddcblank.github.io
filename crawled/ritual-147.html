Goodnight Tincture

Component Cost: See Alchemical Item
Market Price: 750 gp
Key Skill: Nature or Thievery (no check)Level: 6
Category: Poison
Time: 1 hour

This liquid is dissolved into the food or drink of an unsuspecting victim to knock the subject unconscious.

Goodnight Tincture
Level 6 Uncommon

This sweet elixir can incapacitate a foe without ever harming it.

Price: 150 gp
Alchemical Item

Power (Consumable • Sleep): Minor Action. You apply goodnight tincture to an adjacent food or drink. A creature that consumes that food or drink is subject to an attack after 1 minute: +12 vs. Fortitude; on a hit, that creature becomes unconscious for 1 hour or until it is subject to an attack or violent motion.
