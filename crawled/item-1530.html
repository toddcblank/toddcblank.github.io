Power (Poison) Consumable (Minor Action)

Apply heartflow to an adjacent item of food or drink; it retains potency until the end of the encounter. To administer the poison without the target noticing, make a Thievery check against the target's Perception check. A creature that consumes food or drink containing heartflow is subject to an attack: +6 vs. Fortitude; the target takes a -5 penalty to Insight checks and a -2 penalty to Will defense (save ends both).
