Ring of RammingLevel 18 Uncommon

This iron ring is inlaid with the image of a ram's head.

Ring Slot        85,000 gp

Property

When you push a target, you can increase the distance pushed by 1 square.

Power (Force) Daily (Standard Action)

Make an attack: Ranged 10; +21 vs. Fortitude; on a hit, the target takes 3d10 force damage and is pushed 1 square (this distance can be increased by the ring's property). You can instead use this power to make a Strength attack to break down a door or other object using the same attack bonus.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, a hit deals 5d10 force damage and pushes the target 3 squares (which can be increased by the ring's property).



Published in Adventurer's Vault, page(s) 159.
