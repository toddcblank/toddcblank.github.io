Tiamat-Sworn Reaver
Medium natural humanoid dragonborn , dragonborn
Level 18 Skirmisher XP 2000

Initiative +13        Senses Perception +12
HP 176; Bloodied 88 ; see also dragonborn fury and springing assault
Regeneration 10 (only while bloodied).
AC 33; Fortitude 31, Reflex 27, Will 29
Speed 5 see also springing assault

Triple-headed Flail (standard, at-will) Weapon

+23 vs AC; (+24 while bloodied); 1d10 + 5 damage.

Rending Strike (standard; requires triple-headed flail, at-will) Weapon

The Tiamat-sworn reaver attacks up to three targets with its triple-headed flail. If it hits one target, it can push the next target 1 square on a hit, and if it hits two targets, the third takes ongoing 5 damage on a hit (save ends).

Dragonborn Fury (no action; while bloodied)

The reaver gains a +1 racial bonus to attack rolls while bloodied

Skirmish +2d6

If, on its turn, the Tiamat-sworn reaver ends its move at least 3 squares away from where it began its turn, its melee attacks deal 2d6 extra damage until the start of its next turn

Springing Assault (free, when the reaver hits with a melee attack during its turn; recharges when first bloodied)

The reaver shifts 3 squares
