Tenebrous MaskLevel 14 Uncommon

This thin slip of black silk allows you to target foes shrouded from your sight.

Head Slot        21,000 gp

Power Encounter (Minor Action)

Until the end of your next turn, you ignore the -2 penalty to attack rolls against creatures within 10 squares of you that have concealment.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 120.
