Stasis Shell

You render an important object impervious to the rigors of time, encasing it in a smooth, almost imperceptible layer of crystal.

Component Cost: 1,800 gp
Market Price: 9,000 gp
Key Skill: ArcanaLevel: 16
Category: Warding
Time: 1 hour
Duration: Until moved

You perform this ritual on an object in your possession, encasing it in a thin layer of crystal. Until a creature moves the object more than 5 squares in a single round, that layer of unbreakable crystal protects the affected object from all damage and from all effects of age. It is as if time has stopped for the object.

Published in Dragon Magazine 366, page(s) 32.
