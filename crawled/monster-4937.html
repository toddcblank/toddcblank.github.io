Wretched Curse of Baalzebul Aura 1

Any enemy that starts its turn within the aura is affected by the curse of Baalzebul until the end of its next turn. While a creature is affected by the curse of Baalzebul, it takes a -2 penalty to all defenses and gains vulnerable 5 to all damage.
