Anstruth HarpLevel 23 Uncommon

Named for an ancient college of bardic learning, this small, elegant harp holds mighty powers of healing.

Lvl 23+5425,000 gp

Wondrous Item

Enhancement Bonus: attack rolls and damage rolls

Critical: 5d6 extra damage on a critical hit.

Property

Bards can use this item as an implement for bard powers and bard paragon path powers.

Power (Healing) Daily (Standard Action)

Use this power during a short rest. At the end of the short rest, one ally who remained within 20 squares of you during the rest regains all of his or her hit points.

Song of Rest: The ally also regains a healing surge.



Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 210.
