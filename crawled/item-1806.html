Mark of the StarLevel 11 Uncommon

The symbol of the star card from the Deck of Many Things is permanently emblazoned upon your body.

Legendary Boon        0 gp

Power Daily (Free Action)

Choose one ability score. Until the end of the encounter, you gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls made using that ability score, and a +1 bonus to checks with skills that use that ability score.



Published in Madness at Gardmore Abbey, page(s) 1-32.
