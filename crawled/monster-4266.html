Hammerhand (standard, at-will)

Shovel (standard, at-will)

Reach 3; +15 vs AC; 2d8+8 damage (2d8 + 13 while bloodied), and the target is pulled 2 squares.

Automaton Fury Attack (standard, at-will)

The industrial automaton makes two hammerhand attacks and two shovel attacks. Each attack must target a different creature

Fling (immediate reaction, when hit by an attack, at-will)

Reach 3; targets the triggering enemy; +13 vs Reflex; 1d10+5 damage, the automaton slides the target 3 squares and knocks it prone.

Hoist (minor; usable only while the automaton has a creature grabbed, at-will)

Targets grabbed creature; +13 vs Reflex; 2d12+8 damage (2d12 + 13 while bloodied), and the target is restrained (save ends) and no longer grabbed. Only one target can be restrained in this way at a time.
