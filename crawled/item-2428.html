Sending ForkLevel 6 Uncommon

Speaking your voice into this tuning fork casts it farther across the world when used with magic.

Wondrous Item        1,800 gp

Property

You can use this item as an optional focus for the Sending ritual. When you do, multiply the maximum range of the ritual by 10.

Power (Minor Action)

Choose an unoccupied square within 10 squares of you and speak a short phrase (no more than twenty-five words) into the fork. Creatures can hear you as though you are in the chosen square. You do not need line of sight or line of effect to the square.



Published in Dragon Magazine 387, page(s) 29.
