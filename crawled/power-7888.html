Cleric Utility 10Sacred Beneficence

As long as you stand firm against the enemy, your companion draws on your strength to remain unbowed.

Daily        Divine, Healing
Standard Action      Melee touch

Requirement: You must not be bloodied.

Target: One ally

Effect: The target gains regeneration 10 until you are bloodied or until the end of the encounter.
