Attack: Ranged 10 (one creature); +13 vs. Reflex

Attack: Close blast 3 or area burst 3 within 10 (creatures in the blast or burst); +13 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d6 + 4 damage, and ongoing 5 damage (save ends).

Effect: Each ally in the burst gains 5 temporary hit points.

Trigger: The witherer bloodies an enemy or reduces an enemy to 0 hit points or fewer.

Effect (No Action): The witherer gains 1d6 + 3 temporary hit points.
