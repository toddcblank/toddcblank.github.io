Standard Actions

Triggered Actions

Pincers At-Will

Requirement: The klurichir must not have a creature grabbed.

Trigger: An enemy marked by the klurichir moves.

Attack (Immediate Reaction): Melee 2 (the triggering enemy); +31 vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d8 + 13 damage, and the target is grabbed.

    Sustain Minor: The klurichir sustains the grab, and the target takes 2d8 + 13 damage.

Variable Resistance 3/Encounter

Trigger: The klurichir takes acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder damage.

Effect (Free Action): The klurichir gains resist 30 to the triggering damage type until the end of the encounter or until it uses variable resistance again.

Blasphemous Utterance (necrotic) Encounter

Trigger: The klurichir is first bloodied.

Attack (Free Action): Close blast 5 (enemies in the blast); +31 vs. Will

Hit: 3d6 + 8 necrotic damage, and the target is dazed (save ends).
