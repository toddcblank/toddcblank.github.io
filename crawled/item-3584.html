Candle of TomorrowLevel 15 Uncommon

This silver candle gives off a breathtaking light for the brief instant before it and the creatures around it vanish.

Consumable        25,000 gp

Power Consumable Utility (Standard Action)

Effect: You light the candle and specify a period of up to 24 hours. Each willing creature within 2 squares of the candle is removed from play until the time you specified elapses. When a creature reappears, it does so in the space from which it vanished or the nearest unoccupied space.



Published in Dragon Magazine 430.
