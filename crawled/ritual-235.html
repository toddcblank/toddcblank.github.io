Flashbang Powder

Component Cost: see alchemical item Flashbang Powder
Market Price: 500 gpLevel: 8
Category: Volatile
Time: 1 hour

When thrown against a hard surface, this paper packet rips open and exposes the reagents to air, causing them to explode and smoke.

Flashbang Powder
Level 8 Uncommon

The packet explodes with a bright flash and a sharp report, filling the area with smoke and defying multiple senses.

Price: 250 gp
Alchemical Item

Power (Consumable • Zone): Standard Action. Make an attack: Close burst 1; +10 vs. Fortitude; as a free action, you can make a Stealth check against the target with a +4 bonus. If you succeed, you are hidden from that enemy until the end of your turn or until you attack. Creatures that do not rely on sight to detect other creatures are immune to this effect. Effect: The burst creates a zone, which contains smoke that lasts until the end of your next turn. The smoke lightly obscures the area.
