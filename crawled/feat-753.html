Beast Protector

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Ranger, Beast Mastery class feature
Benefit: If an adjacent enemy makes a melee attack against your beast companion, doing so provokes an opportunity attack from you. If you are adjacent to your beast companion, you can make this attack even if you can’t reach the attacker (you attack the attacker’s reaching limb, for instance).
