Vanous's Combined AwarenessLevel 5 Uncommon

Your companion's guttural growl alerts you to danger.

Grandmaster Training        1,000 gp

Requirement: You must have the beast mastery or beast mastery (hybrid) class feature.

Property

While you are within 5 squares of your beast companion, you gain a +2 item bonus to Perception checks.

Power Daily (No Action)

Requirement: You must be within 5 squares of your beast companion.

Trigger: You would make an initiative check.

Effect: Roll for your initiative check twice and use the higher result.



Published in Dragon Magazine 393.
