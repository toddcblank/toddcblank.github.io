Ranger Attack 29Huntmaster's Mauling

You slice the flesh, and your beast companion tears it open.

Daily        Beast, Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon (beast 1)

Target: One creature

Primary Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Strength modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: Your beast companion makes a secondary attack against the target. If the primary attack bloodied the target, the beast gains combat advantage for the secondary attack.

  Secondary Attack: Beast’s attack bonus vs. AC

  Hit: 5[B] + beast’s Strength modifier damage.

  Miss: Half damage.

  Beast: If your companion is a bear, a snake, or a wolf, the secondary attack deals extra damage equal to your Wisdom modifier.
