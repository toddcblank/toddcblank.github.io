Underchasm Darkwatcher Attack 20Form of the Umber Hulk

Your eyes take on an insectlike appearance, you sprout antennae from atop your head, and a pair of sharp mandibles grows out from your mouth. At the moment you choose, you can gaze upon your enemies and sow confusion in their ranks.

Daily        Polymorph, Primal
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You assume the guardian form of the umber hulk. While you are in this form, you gain a +2 power bonus to AC and Fortitude defense. In addition, you also gain tremorsense 5 and a burrow speed of 2.
Once during this encounter, you can use the Form of the Umber Hulk Attack power while you are in this form.


Form of the Umber Hulk Attack

Encounter      Polymorph, Primal
Standard Action      Close blast 3

Target: Each enemy in the blast

Attack: Constitution or Charisma vs. Will

Hit: Target slides 1 square and is dazed (save ends).

Miss: Target slides 1 square.

Requirement: The power Form of the Umber Hulk must be active in order to use this power.
