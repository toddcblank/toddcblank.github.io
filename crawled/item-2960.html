Unseen Servant's HandLevel 4 Common

This barely visible glass hand improves your magical, invisible work force.

Wondrous Item        840 gp

Property

You can use this item as a focus for the Unseen Servant ritual in place of the standard focus. When you do, the ritual conjures two unseen servants with each casting, and each servant conjured by the ritual can lift up to 300 pounds.



Published in Dragon Magazine 387, page(s) 30.
