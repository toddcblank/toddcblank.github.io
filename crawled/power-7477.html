Monk Attack 17Steps of Grasping Fire [Attack Technique]

You become sheathed in flames and leave a trail of fire behind you with each step.

Encounter        Fire, Full Discipline, Implement, Psionic
Standard Action      Close blast 3

Target: Each creature in the blast

Attack: Dexterity vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d10 + Dexterity modifier fire damage.


Steps of Grasping Fire [Movement Technique]

You become sheathed in flames and leave a trail of fire behind you with each step.

Encounter      Fire, Full Discipline, Psionic
Move Action      Personal

Effect: You move your speed. During this movement, any enemy that hits you takes fire damage equal to 5 + your Wisdom modifier, and each square you leave is filled with fire until the end of your next turn. Any creature that enters a fire square or starts its turn there takes fire damage equal to 5 + your Wisdom modifier. A creature can take this damage only once per turn.
