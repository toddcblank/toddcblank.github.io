The mordant hydra makes six basic attacks (any combination of bite attacks and acid spit attacks).

Each time the mordant hydra becomes dazed or stunned, it loses one attack on its next turn instead. Multiple such effects stack.

The mordant hydra can make opportunity attacks against all enemies within its reach (3 squares).
