Monk Attack 1Stunning Palm

Your psionic energy keeps you focused in combat, and can be released in a stunning blow that knocks a foe senseless.

Daily        Implement, Psionic, Stance
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: Until the stance ends, you cannot be dazed or stunned, and you can use the Stunning Palm Attack power.


Stunning Palm Attack

Daily      Implement, Psionic, Stance
Standard Action      Melee touch

Requirement: The power Stunning Palm must be active in order to use this power.

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. Fortitude

Hit: Dexterity modifier damage, and the target is stunned (save ends).

Miss: 2d8 + Dexterity modifier damage.

Effect: The stance ends.


Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 62.
