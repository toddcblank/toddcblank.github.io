Bolster Object

Though it looks no different, the iron portcullis has become harder than steel. The ram can batter itself to flinders before the portcullis gives way.

Component Cost: 150 gp
Market Price: 300 gp
Key Skill: Arcana or NatureLevel: 6
Category: Warding
Time: 10 minutes
Duration: Instantaneous

The target object, which you must touch during the performance of the ritual, increases its hit points by 10% for every 5 points you have on your check result. An object targeted by this ritual multiple times benefits only from the best casting.

Published in Dragon Magazine 366, page(s) 27.
