Blackleaf GlovesLevel 4 Uncommon

Made from the leaves of an ancient Feywild oak, these wellpreserved black gloves increase the utility of your pact boon.

Hands Slot        840 gp

Power (Teleportation) Encounter (Free Action)

Trigger: Your pact boon triggers.

Effect: You teleport 3 squares in addition to the other effect of your pact boon.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 108.
