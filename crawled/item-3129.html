Robe of the ArchmageLevel 20+ Rare

You can tap this robe’s power to broaden your magical ability, but at a cost, since your body acts as a conduit for that force.

Lvl 20+4125,000 gp Lvl 30+63,125,000 gp
Lvl 25+5625,000 gp

Armor: Cloth

Enhancement Bonus: AC

Properties

  • You gain a +1 item bonus to Fortitude and Will.
        Level 25 or 30: The bonus increases to +2.
  • After each extended rest, you can prepare one additional utility power of your level or lower from your spellbook. You must have a spellbook to use this benefit, and the additional power can be the same level as another utility power you prepare.

Utility Power Daily (Minor Action)

Effect: You take damage equal to your bloodied value that can’t be reduced in any way. The next damage roll you make with an arcane attack power before the end of the turn has all of its damage dice maximized.
