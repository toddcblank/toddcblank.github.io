Animated Armory
Terrain
Level 8 TrapXP 1750

Detect Perception DC 24

Initiative see below

Triggered Actions

Animate Objects Encounter

Trigger: A living creature enters the room with the trap.

Effect: The weapons and suits of armor animate and attack.

Countermeasures

Disable: Arcana DC 24 (standard action). Requirement: The character must have line of sight to an animated object. Success: One animated object of the character’s choice drops to 0 hit points, and another animated object cannot take actions or move until the end of its next turn. Failure (5 or more): All animated objects in the room gain +2 to attack rolls until the end of each object’s next turn. Special: After four successful Arcana checks, the trap is disabled for 1 hour.

Destroy: Dropping every animated object to 0 hit points destroys the trap.

Published in Dungeon Magazine 214.
