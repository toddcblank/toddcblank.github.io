Level 11 DiseaseMournland Affliction

Afflicted by the Mournland, a victim of this disease acts as one without hope, sallow, wide-eyed, and despairing.

Stage 0: The target recovers from the disease.
Stage 1: Initial Effect: The target’s healing surge value is reduced to half of its full normal value.
Stage 2: The target’s healing surge value is reduced to half of its full normal value. The target loses two healing surges. He or she can’t regain these lost healing surges until he or she is cured.
Stage 3: The target can’t spend healing surges.
Check: At the end of each extended rest, the target makes an Endurance check if it is at stage 1 or 2.
16 or lower: The stage of the disease increases by one.
17-21: No Change
22 or higher: The stage of the disease decreases by one.

Published in Eberron Campaign Setting, page(s) 93.
