Sorcerer Utility 16Thunder Buffer

Distant thunder grumbles around you, and you can unleash it against an attacker to knock the creature backward.

Daily        Arcane
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, you gain a +2 power bonus to AC, and as an immediate reaction you can push each enemy that hits you with a melee attack a number of squares equal to your Charisma modifier.


Published in Arcane Power, page(s) 36.
