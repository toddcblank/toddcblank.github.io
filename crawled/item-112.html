Wayfinder Badge

This simple gold brooch has a shape that indicates your affiliation with a secret society.

Wondrous Item

Property

Epic: You gain a +2 bonus to Perception checks against aberrant creatures, as well as a + 2 bonus to saving throws against any effect an aberrant creature imposes on you.

Power Encounter (Standard Action)

Heroic: You can send a short message to a person you know who possesses a Wayfinder badge attuned to yours, as long as the target is on the same plane.
Paragon: The power becomes at-will, and the target need not be on the same plane.
Epic: All primal spirits the wearer knows of are considered to have attuned Wayfinder badges and are thus eligible targets for this power.

Power (Teleportation) At-Will (Free Action)

Paragon: Triggered if you fall off Hand of Fate. You arrive on the deck of Hand of Fate in a square of your choice.

Power (Teleportation) Daily (Move Action)

Paragon: An allied character can trigger it if you are unconscious or helpless. The ally can trigger this effect even if he or she lacks line of sight or line of effect to you, and from up to 1 mile away. You teleport to the deck of Hand of Fate in a square of your choice.
