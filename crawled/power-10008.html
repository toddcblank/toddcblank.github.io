Fighter Attack 25Time to Die

You’ ve battled this enemy long enough: Putting all your strength behind the attack, you fling the foe away and then leap after it to bring the battle to a bloody close.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a two-handed weapon.

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage, and you push the target a number of squares equal to your Constitution modifier.

Miss: Half damage, and you push the target 2 squares.

Effect: You make a charge attack against the same target as a free action. If this attack hits, in addition to the damage from the attack, you push the target a number of squares equal to your Constitution modifier and your target falls prone.


Revision (11/5/2009)
Page 65. Time to Die’s effect was changed to say “You make a charge attack against the same target as a free action. If this attack hits, in addition to the damage from the attack, you push the target a number of squares equal to your Constitution modifier and your target falls prone.”

Published in Dragon Magazine 379, page(s) 65.
