Dythan's Legion Dragoon
Medium natural humanoid, dragonborn
Level 12 Controller (Leader) XP 700

HP 119; Bloodied 59Initiative +8
AC 26, Fortitude 25, Reflex 24, Will 24Perception+8
Speed 6

Traits

Masterful Rider

Whenever the dragoon is mounted and subjected to a pull, push, or slide, it can move 1 square fewer than the effect specifies.

Standard Actions

Lance (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 2 (one creature); +17 vs. AC. The dragoon has a +2 bonus to hit while bloodied.

Hit: 2d12 + 7 damage. If the dragoon is mounted, the target also falls prone.

Minor Actions

Battlefield Mastery Recharge

Effect: Close burst 2 (allies in the burst). Each target can shift 1 square as a free action.

Dragon Breath (acid) Encounter

Attack: Close blast 3 (creatures in the blast); +15 vs. Reflex. The dragoon has a +2 bonus to hit while bloodied.

Hit: 1d6 + 6 acid damage.

Triggered Actions

Incensed Mount At-Will

Trigger: An enemy’s attack damages the dragoon while the dragoon is mounted.

Effect (Immediate Reaction): Melee 1 (the dragoon’s mount). The target can make a basic attack against the triggering enemy as a free action.
