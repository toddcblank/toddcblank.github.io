Dantrag's BracersLevel 18 Uncommon

These wide bracers are lined with gleaming mithral rings.

Arms Slot        85,000 gp

Properties

  • This item can be worn in the arms slot or the feet slot.
  • Unique; this item cannot be purchased or created with the Enchant Magic Item ritual.
  • You can draw or sheathe a weapon, or draw or stow an item, once per round as a free action.

Power

Requirement: You must be using Dantrag's bracers in the arms slot. Standard Action. Make a melee basic attack against one or two creatures.

Power

Requirement: You must be using Dantrag's bracers in the feet slot. Move Action. Shift a number of squares equal to half your speed.
