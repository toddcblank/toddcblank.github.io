Godsworn Sidestep

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Avenger, must worship Corellon
Benefit: You gain a +2 feat bonus to Religion checks. In addition, whenever you score a critical hit, you can teleport 5 squares to any unoccupied square adjacent to your oath of enmity target as a godsworn boon. You can benefit from only one godsworn boon as a result of any given critical hit.

Published in Dragon Magazine 384, page(s) 43.
