Storm Pillar (lightning) Aura 1

Any enemy that willingly enters a square in the aura takes 5 lightning damage. A creature can take this damage only once per turn.

Insubstantial

The pillar takes half damage from any damage source, except force damage and psychic damage. Whenever the pillar takes psychic damage, it loses this trait until the start of its next turn.

Ooze

While squeezing, the pillar moves at full speed rather than half speed, it doesn’t take the -5 penalty to attack rolls, and it doesn’t grant combat advantage for squeezing.
