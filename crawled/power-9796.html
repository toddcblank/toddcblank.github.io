Everflame Guardian Attack 20Elder Flame

You call forth a living piece of Everflame and set it against your enemies.

Daily        Conjuration, Fire, Implement, Primal
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Effect: You conjure a shard of Everflame in an unoccupied square within range. The shard lasts until the end of the encounter. When any ally within 2 squares of the shard hits an enemy, the attack deals 1d10 extra fire damage to that enemy. As a move action, you can move the shard 5 squares. You can use the Elder Flame Attack power, centered on the shard. Doing so destroys the shard.


Elder Flame Attack

Encounter      Conjuration, Fire, Implement, Primal
Minor Action      Close burst 2

Target: Each enemy in the burst

Attack: Wisdom vs. Will

Hit: 4d8 + Wisdom modifier fire damage.

Requirement: The Elder Flame power must be active for you to use this power.
