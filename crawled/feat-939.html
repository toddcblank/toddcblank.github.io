Highborn Drow

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, drow
Benefit: You gain a +2 feat bonus to Arcana checks. In addition, whenever you use your Lolthtouched racial feature, you can use the cloud of darkness, darkfire, or webs of darkness power.


Feat AttackWebs of Darkness

Inky tendrils of solidified darkness leap from your fingertip and bind your enemies.

Encounter
Standard Action      Close blast 3

Target: Each creature in the blast

Attack: Intelligence + 4 vs. Reflex, Wisdom + 4 vs. Reflex, or Charisma + 4 vs. Reflex
Increase to +6 bonus at 21st level

Effect: Until the end of your next turn, the target is slowed and all creatures have concealment against the target.


Published in Dragon Magazine 367, page(s) 38.
