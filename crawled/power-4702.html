Pack Runner Attack 20Neck-Bite Pounce

Your wolf friend springs at your adversary, bowling the foe over and landing a vicious bite on a vital area.

Daily        Beast, Martial
Standard Action      Melee beast 1

Target: One creature

Attack: Beast’s attack bonus vs. AC

Hit: 3[B] + beast’s Dexterity modifier damage, and the target is knocked prone and takes ongoing 10 damage (save ends).

Miss: No damage, and the target is slowed and takes ongoing 10 damage (save ends both).


Published in Martial Power, page(s) 65.
