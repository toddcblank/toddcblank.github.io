Roc ArmorLevel 18+ Uncommon

This armor is decorated with roc feathers. When you enter beast form, you can tap into the great beast's spirit to adopt its shape.

Armor: Hide

Enhancement Bonus: AC

Power Daily (Free Action)

Until the end of the encounter, you can use your wild shape power to assume the form of a roc that is your size. In this form, you gain a fly speed of 6, and you can hover. You can't use daily attack powers or manipulate objects.

Until this power ends, you can use wild shape to change among this form, another beast form, and your humanoid form.
