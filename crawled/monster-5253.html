Immune disease, poison; Resist 20 necrotic, insubstantial; Vulnerable 10 radiant
Saving Throws +5; Action Points 2 ; *Darkness and Crippling Weakness are granted to Moghadam by his possession of Ruinblade

Traits

Dread Vengeance (psychic) Aura 2

While Moghadam is not bloodied, any enemy that starts its turn within the aura takes 2d10 psychic damage and is dazed until the start of its next turn.

Spawn Wraith

Any humanoid killed by Moghadam rises as a free-willed sword wraith (page 63) at the start of its creator’s next turn, appearing in the space where it died, or in the nearest unoccupied space if that space is occupied. Raising the slain creature (using a Raise Dead ritual) does not destroy the spawned wraith.

Standard Actions

Blade of Vengeance (necrotic, psychic) At-Will

Requirement: Moghadam must be wielding a greatsword.

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +24 vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d10 necrotic and psychic damage, and ongoing 10 psychic damage (save ends).

Blade Storm At-Will

Requirement: Moghadam must be wielding a greatsword.

Effect: Moghadam uses blade of vengeance four times, but no more than twice against a single target. Against a blinded target, each attack deals 1d10 extra damage.

Darkness (zone) At-Will

Attack: Close burst 5 (enemies in the burst); +24 vs. Reflex

Hit: The burst creates a zone that lasts until the end of Moghadam’s next turn. Enemies within the zone are blinded.

Mind Wrack (psychic) Recharge

Attack: Close blast 5 (enemies in the blast); +24 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 4d8 + 14 psychic damage, and the target gains vulnerable 10 psychic (save ends).

Triggered Actions

Crippling Weakness Daily

Trigger: Moghadam scores a critical hit with blade of vengeance.

Effect (Free Action): The target is weakened (save ends). While it is weakened, the target cannot deal ongoing damage.

Indestructible

Trigger: Moghadam drops to 0 hit points.

Effect (No Action): Moghadam’s body fades into shadow, but he is not destroyed. Unless Ruinblade bonds to a new wielder, Moghadam reappears in 1d10 days within 1 square of the sword.
