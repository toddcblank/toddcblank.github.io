Najid's War Camel
Large natural beast (mount)
Level 5 Brute XP 200

Initiative +4        Senses Perception +9; low-light vision
HP 78; Bloodied 39
AC 17; Fortitude 18, Reflex 17, Will 16
Speed 8 (earth walk)

Kick (standard, at-will)

+8 vs AC; 1d6+6 damage.

Trample (standard, at-will)

The war camel can move up to its speed and enter enemies’ spaces. This movement provokes opportunity attacks, and the war camel must end its movement in an unoccupied space. When it enters an enemy’s space, the war camel makes a trample attack; +6 vs Reflex; 1d6+7 damage, and the target is knocked prone.

Charger (while mounted by a friendly rider of 5th level or higher; at-will)

The war camel grants its rider a +5 bonus to damage rolls on charge attacks.
