Echoes of ProvidenceLevel 18+ Uncommon

Faint whispers speak of knowledge and events both ancient and prescient.

Lvl 1885,000 gp Lvl 282,125,000 gp

Divine Boon

Properties

  • You cannot be deafened.
  • You gain blindsight 1 (2 while you are blinded).
        Level 28: You gain blindsight 2 (5 while you are blinded).

Utility Power Daily (No Action)

Trigger: You make an ability check or a skill check.

Effect: Roll a d6. On a 3 or higher, treat the d20 result as if you rolled a 20 plus the result on the d6.

Level 28: Treat the d20 result as if you rolled a 20 plus twice the result on the d6.
