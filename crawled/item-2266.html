Ring of the RisenLevel 23 Uncommon

This simple silver ring makes it easier for your allies to revive you.

Ring Slot        425,000 gp

Property

When you have 0 hit points or fewer and spend a healing surge, you regain 2d6 additional hit points.

Power (Healing) Daily (No Action)

Trigger: You spend a healing surge as a result of an ally's action.

Effect: You spend another healing surge.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, you don't spend another healing surge. You instead regain hit points as if you had spent one.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 72.
