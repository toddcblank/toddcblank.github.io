Deathgaunt Lasher
Medium aberrant humanoid (undead)
Level 5 Soldier XP 200

Initiative +8        Senses Perception +2; low-light vision
HP 63; Bloodied 31
AC 21; Fortitude 16, Reflex 18, Will 16
Immune disease, poison; Resist 5 necrotic; Vulnerable 5 radiant
Speed 8

Tentacle (standard, at-will)

+12 vs AC; 1d6 + 4 damage, and the target is marked until the end of the deathgaunt lasher’s next turn. Reach 3

Tentacle Lash (standard, at-will) Necrotic

Reach 3; +12 vs AC; 1d10 + 4 damage, and the target is dazed and marked until the end of the lasher’s next turn. targets a creature marked by the deathgaunt lasher.

Deathgaunt Kiss (standard, at-will) Necrotic

+12 vs AC; 2d8 + 4 necrotic damage, and ongoing 5 necrotic damage. Targets a dazed, stunned, or unconscious creature.

Marked Threatening Reach

A deathgaunt lasher can make opportunity attacks against all enemies within its reach (3 squares) that it has marked.
