Entrancing Mystic Attack 11Hekiah's Trance

Your foe becomes lost in your eyes, shuffling in the direction of your gaze.

Encounter        Arcane, Charm, Implement, Psychic
Standard Action      Ranged 5

Target: One creature

Attack: Charisma vs. Will

Hit: 1d8 + Charisma modifier psychic damage, and you slide the target a number of squares equal to your Charisma modifier. The target is slowed until the end of your next turn.
