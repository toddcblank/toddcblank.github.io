Divine Boon

Property

You gain a +1 item bonus to saving throws against the slowed, immobilized, and restrained conditions.
    Level 8: Gain a +2 item bonus to saving
    Level 13: Gain a +3 item bonus to saving
    Level 18: Gain a +4 item bonus to saving
    Level 23: Gain a +5 item bonus to saving
    Level 28: Gain a +6 item bonus to saving

Power Daily (Immediate Reaction)

Trigger: You become slowed, immobilized or restrained.

Effect: You teleport 2 squares and the triggering effect ends.

Level 8: 3 squares.

Level 13: 4 squares.

Level 18: 5 squares.

Level 23: 6 squares.

Level 28: 7 squares.
