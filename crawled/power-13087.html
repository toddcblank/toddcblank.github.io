Battlemind Utility 22Mental Haven

By opening a portal into your own mind, you are temporarily shunted out of the world, then invigorated on your return.

Daily        Healing, Psionic
Minor Action      Personal

Effect: You are removed from play. At the end of your next turn, you regain hit points equal to your bloodied value or you regain the use of an encounter power you have already used in this encounter. You then reappear in any unoccupied space within 5 squares of the space you last occupied.


Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 45.
