Teleportation DiskLevel 14 Uncommon

Usually set in the floor, this large, ornately inscribed teleportation circle moves you quickly around your domicile.

Wondrous Item        21,000 gp

Power (Teleportation) (Move Action)

When you speak a command word while standing on the disk, you teleport to any location in the same residence or structure as a move action.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 83.
