Description: Known for their brutish appearance, one-eyed ravers are tactical geniuses and leaders of fomorian warbands. In peacetime, they organize raiding parties of cyclopses and evil fey. In war, they head vast armies, where their tactical acumen shifts the tides of battle.

Wielding massive axes and clad in elaborate cyclops-forged armor, one-eyed ravers channel the loathing their race bears for other creatures into their blows.
