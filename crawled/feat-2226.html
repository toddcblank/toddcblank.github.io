Horns of the Shadow Reaper

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Minotaur, assassin, assassin’s shroud power
Benefit: When you charge and use your goring charge racial power you and target has at least one of your assassin’s shrouds on it, you gain a +2 bonus to your attack roll and, after resolving the attack, you can shift half your speed as part of the charge.
