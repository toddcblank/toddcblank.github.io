Elixir of PhasingLevel 17 Uncommon

This brackish gray liquid seems to appear and disappear within its vial at short intervals. The effect carries over to you when you consume it.

Consumable: Elixir        2,600 gp

Utility Power Consumable (Minor Action)

Effect: You drink the elixir and are phasing until the end of your next turn.



Published in Mordenkainen's Magnificent Emporium, page(s) 94.
