Claw (standard, at-will)

Reach 2; +36 vs AC; 3d8+5 damage, and the target is slowed until the end of its next turn.

Chaos Strike (standard, at-will)

The black slaad overlord shifts 3 squares and makes two claw attacks. If both attacks hit the same target, the target loses a healing surge

Ranged 20; +34 vs Reflex; 3d10+6 damage, and the overlord teleports to within 5 squares of the target.

Area burst 2 within 10; +34 vs Fortitude; ongoing 10 necrotic damage, and the target is slowed (save ends both).

Zone of Oblivion ( when the overlord drops to 0 hit points, ) Zone

Close burst 2; +34 vs Reflex; 4d10 damage. The burst becomes a black void until the end of the encounter. The zone blocks line of sight. Any creature that enters the zone or starts its turn there takes 3d10 + 6 damage.
