Rod of the Hidden StarLevel 8+ Uncommon

Formed of iridescent stone drawn from the Far Realm, this rod enhances the boon from the star pact, increasing your own powers and aiding allies as well.

Implement: Rod

Enhancement Bonus: attack rolls and damage rolls

Power Daily (Free Action)

Use this power when your star pact boon triggers. All allies within a number of squares equal to the enhancement bonus of this rod gain a +1 bonus on any one d20 roll until the end of your next turn.

Power Daily (Free Action)

Use this power when your star pact boon triggers. Add the enhancement bonus of this rod to the bonus your pact gives you.
