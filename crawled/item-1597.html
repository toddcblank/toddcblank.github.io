Illusionist's GlovesLevel 9 Uncommon

These shimmering gloves enhance your illusion spells and let you quickly alter an illusion that would otherwise be ineffective.

Hands Slot        4,200 gp

Property

When you hit an enemy granting combat advantage to you with an illusion attack power, that enemy takes a –2 penalty to saving throws against the power’s effects.

Power Daily (Free Action)

Trigger: You miss with an illusion attack power.

Effect: You reroll the attack roll and must use the second result.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 60.
