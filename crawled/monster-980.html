Bite (standard, at-will)

+31 vs AC; 1d12+10 damage.

Bite Flurry (standard, at-will)

Close blast 2; +30 vs AC; 1d12+10 damage.

First Strike

A haraevor has combat advantage against any creature that has not yet acted in an encounter.

Combat Advantage

The haraevor deals an extra 3d10 damage with attacks against any target it has combat advantage against.

Four Heads

A haraevor rolls initiative four times (once for each head) and uses the highest result. A haraevor loses a head whenever it suffers a critical hit. If it is reduced to no heads, it instantly dies. Heads never grow back.
