Verdant Stalker Attack 11Shadow to Shadow

You slide behind cover, attack your foe from a distance, and then slip back into the shadows again.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Ranged weapon

Requirement: You must be using a hand crossbow, a repeating crossbow, a shortbow, a shuriken, or a sling.

Effect: Before and after the attack, you shift 3 squares. After each shift, you can make a Stealth check to become hidden.

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Dexterity modifier damage.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 76.
