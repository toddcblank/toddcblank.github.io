Cognizance RingLevel 16 Uncommon

Inlaid with tourmaline gems, this electrum trinket strengthens your mind and spirit.

Ring Slot        45,000 gp

Property

Gain a +1 item bonus to saving throws against conditions with the charm, fear, illusion, or psychic keyword.

Power Daily (Minor Action)

Gain a +5 power bonus to saving throws against conditions with the charm, fear, illusion, or psychic keyword until the end of the encounter.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, you also gain a +2 power bonus to Will defense against powers with those keywords.
