Immurement of the Abandoned ThroneLevel 24 Rare

This marred bronze scepter has had the sigil of the kingdom it once represented scratched off its surface.

Consumable        21,000 gp

Power Consumable (Standard Action)

You expend this immurement and change the terrain in a close blast 4 until the end of the encounter. Replace the terrain in the blast with the following terrain and effects.

*: The dais is 1 square higher than the ground. Entering the dais from the ground costs 1 extra square of movement.

*: Any creature that starts its turn within 2 squares of the throne is dazed until the end of its turn.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 90.
