Traits

Aquatic

The murklord can breathe underwater. In aquatic combat, it gains a +2 bonus to attack rolls against nonaquatic creatures.

Standard Actions

Bite At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +7 vs. AC

Hit: 1d6 + 5 damage, or 1d12 + 5 while the murklord is bloodied.

Leapfrog Recharge

Effect: The murklord shifts 3 squares, makes the following attack, and shifts 3 squares again.

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +5 vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d6 + 4 damage, or 2d12 + 4 while the murklord is bloodied.

Minor Actions

Tongue Lash At-Will

Attack: Melee 2 (one creature); +5 vs. Reflex

Hit: The murklord slides the target 1 square.
