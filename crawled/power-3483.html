Spellscarred Attack 3Scar-Carving Blade

Blisters rise on your sword hand and burst, spilling forth redblue burning blood that crawls up your blade as you strike.

Encounter        Arcane, Fire, Necrotic, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC, Dexterity vs. AC, or Constitution vs. AC

Hit: 1[W] + Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution modifier damage, and the target is smeared with your burning spellscarred blood. Your next successful attack against
the target before the end of your next turn causes 5 extra fire and necrotic damage.
