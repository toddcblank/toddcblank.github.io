Corrosive Oil

Component Cost: See below.
Market Price: 400 gp
Key Skill: Arcana or Nature (no check)Level: 3
Category: Oil
Time: 15 minutes

The oil made from the blood of a rust monster can coat any nonmetallic surface. Any metallic weapon that comes into contact with the coated surface begins to lose its effectiveness.

Corrosive Oil
Level 3 Uncommon

Nonmetallic armor coated with this oil has the ability to lessen the effectiveness of metallic weapons that strike it.

Price: 30 gp
Alchemical Item

Power (Consumable): Standard Action. Apply corrosive oil to a set of nonmetallic armor that you wear. The next time a creature hits you with a metallic weapon, make the following attack: +6 vs. Reflex; on a hit, the attacker takes a –1 penalty to attack rolls with that weapon and a –2 penalty to damage rolls with that weapon (save ends both). Aftereffect: The attacker takes a –2 penalty to damage rolls made with that weapon (save ends).
