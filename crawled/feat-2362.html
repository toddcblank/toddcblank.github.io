Midnight Blade Whisperer [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Rogue, Midnight Blade Student feat
Benefit: When you are attacking with a melee light blade and you have proficiency with that weapon, you gain the following two benefits.
Whenever you hit an enemy with a martial encounter or daily power during your turn, you increase the distance you shift by 1 square until the end of your turn.
When you attack with a power associated with this feat, after the attack you can use a free action to shift 2 squares and make a Stealth check to become hidden.


Associated Powers:
Blindside: (Only when used as a melee attack)Blistering Outburst, Cunning Cyclone, Dismaying Slash,
Perfect Strike: (Only when used as a melee attack)
Trickster's Blade: (Only when used as a melee attack)
