Suppression Crystal

Component Cost: See below
Market Price: 120 gp
Key Skill: Arcana or Nature (no check)Level: 4
Category: Other
Time: 1 hour

A suppression crystal catches sound and energy, trapping them and safely bleeding them.

Suppression Crystal
Level 4 Uncommon

The crystal diminishes the lightning bolt and silences the thunder.

Price: 40 gp
Alchemical Item

Power (Consumable): Minor Action. Until it triggers or until the end of the encounter, you are protected by the suppression crystal. As an immediate interrupt that occurs automatically when you are first hit by a thunder or a lightning attack, you gain resist 5 thunder and resist 5 lightning until the end of your next turn. If this power is used but the resistance is not triggered before the end of the encounter, the crystal is still consumed.
