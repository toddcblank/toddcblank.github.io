Gem-Studded IdolLevel 21 Uncommon

This golden statue of a rampant dragon has sockets where its eyes would be and at the ends of its horns and claws.

Wondrous Item        225,000 gp

Property

While five different gemstones each worth at least 10,000 gp are displayed in this idol and you are within 10 squares of the idol, you gain a +5 bonus to Diplomacy, Intimidate, and Insight checks.



Published in Dragon Magazine 383, page(s) 47.
