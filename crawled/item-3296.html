Dust of DisappearanceLevel 8 Uncommon

When you sprinkle this dust over yourself, you disappear from sight until you do something to shake it loose.

Consumable        125 gp

Utility Power (Illusion) Consumable (Minor Action)

Effect: You sprinkle this dust over yourself or an adjacent ally. That character becomes invisible until he or she moves or attacks, or until the end of the encounter.



Published in Mordenkainen's Magnificent Emporium, page(s) 98.
