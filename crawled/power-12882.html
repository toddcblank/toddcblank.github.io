Shaman Utility 22Sand Summons

You dismiss your spirit companion, using its power to cause sand to boil up from the ground. The sands consume your allies and deliver them to safety while leaving an area of unstable terrain.

Daily        Primal, Spirit, Teleportation, Zone
Minor Action      Close burst spirit 2

Target: Each ally in the burst

Effect: Your spirit companion disappears. You teleport each target 5 squares. The burst creates a zone of difficult terrain that lasts until the end of the encounter.


Published in Dark Sun Campaign Setting, page(s) 93.
