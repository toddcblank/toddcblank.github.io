Spirit Master of Lalali-Puy [Greater Pact]

Prerequisite: Warlock, Sorcerer-King Pact class feature, Spirit Talker of Lalali-Puy feat
Benefit: In addition to regaining the use of your fell might, whenever you drop an enemy you have cursed to 0 hit points, you slide each enemy adjacent to you and each enemy cursed by you 1 square. No enemy can be slid more than 1 square by this effect.
    You can also expend your fell might to augment any power associated with this feat. When you hit an enemy with an associated power augmented by this feat, the target makes a basic attack against a legal target you choose.


Associated Powers: Bewitching Whispers, Curse of the Fey King, Eldritch Rain, Mire the Mind, Thirsting Tendrils, Thorns of Venom

Published in Dragon Magazine 390.
