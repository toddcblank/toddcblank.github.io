Power Surge

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, psion
Benefit: You can expend the use of an unused daily psion attack power to regain 2 power points. If the daily power is 15th level or higher, you instead regain 4 power points. If the daily power is 25th level or higher, you instead regain 6 power points.

Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 138.
