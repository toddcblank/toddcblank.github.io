Fly speed

A creature that has a fly speed, such as an angel or a dragon, can move through the air. See “Flying”.

Published in Monster Manual 2, page(s) 217, Monster Manual, page(s) 281, Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 220, Divine Power, page(s) 159, Rules Compendium, page(s) 311, Monster Vault: Threats to the Nentir Vale, page(s) 123.
