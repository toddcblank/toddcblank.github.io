Traits

Whirling Winds Aura 1

The dragon has partial concealment against any enemy in the aura.

Standard Actions

Bite (cold) At-Will

Attack: Melee 2 (one creature); +13 vs. AC

Hit: 1d8 + 5 damage plus 2d6 cold damage.

Claw (lightning) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +13 vs. AC

Hit: 2d4 + 5 damage, and ongoing 5 lightning damage (save ends).

Double Attack

Effect: The dragon uses bite and claw or uses claw twice.

Minor Actions

Expanding Aura Recharge when the dragon starts its turn and its whirling winds is smaller than aura 5

Effect: The whirling winds aura expands by 2 squares.

Twister Unleashed Recharge when the dragon starts its turn and its whirling winds is aura 5

Effect: Any effect on the dragon that includes the immobilized, restrained, or slowed condition ends. The dragon becomes insubstantial and shifts up to its speed, and it can move through enemies’ spaces. Each time the dragon enters an enemy’s space for the first time during this movement, it can use bite against that enemy. On a hit, the dragon slides the target up to 2 squares and the target falls prone.

Effect: The dragon’s whirling winds reverts to aura 1.

Triggered Actions

Storm Twist At-Will

Trigger: The dragon takes lightning or thunder damage.

Effect (Opportunity Action): The dragon becomes insubstantial and can shift up to its speed.
