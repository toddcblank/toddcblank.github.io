Bard Attack 19All Soul's Ball

A haunting tune fills the air, drawing the souls of the dying to dance among their killers.

Daily        Arcane, Implement, Necrotic, Zone
Standard Action      Area burst 2 within 10

Target: Each enemy in the burst

Attack: Charisma vs. Will

Hit: 3d8 + Charisma modifier necrotic damage, and the target is immobilized until the end of its next turn.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: The burst creates a zone that lasts until the end of the encounter. When an enemy within the zone is reduced to 0 hit points or fewer, allies adjacent to the enemy may immediately spend a healing surge as a free action, and enemies adjacent to the dying enemy grant combat advantage until the end of their next turn.


Published in Dragon Magazine 372, page(s) 21.
