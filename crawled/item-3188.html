Properties

  • You gain darkvision.

Utility Power Encounter (Minor Action)

Effect: You gain a +2 item bonus to your next attack roll before the end of your next turn. If that attack hits, you gain an item bonus to its damage roll. The bonus equals 4 + the tome’s enhancement bonus. If you do not kill a creature before the end of your next turn, you take damage equal to your healing surge value at the end of that turn. If this damage drops you below 1 hit point, you die.
