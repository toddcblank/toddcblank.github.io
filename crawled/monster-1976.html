Effect: The nighthaunt shrine creates a zone in an area burst 1 within 10 that lasts until the end of the shrine’s next turn. Squares within the zone are totally obscured and block line of sight for creatures that do not have darkvision. A creature that starts its turn within the zone takes 10 necrotic damage.

Sustain Minor: The zone lasts until the end of the shrine’s next turn. In addition, it can use a move action to move the zone 5 squares.
