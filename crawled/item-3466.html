Glowing GlobeLevel 3 Rare

Unless ordered to remain in a particular spot, this luminous glass globe floats a handspan above the creature that last touched it, giving that creature access to its powers.

Wondrous Item        680 gp

Property

If the glowing globe is brought within 1 square of another glowing globe, you cannot activate any of its powers and it goes dark.

Utility Power At-Will (Minor Action)

Effect: The globe sheds dim light in a 2-square radius, bright light in a 10-square radius, or stops shedding light.

Utility Power Daily (Minor Action)

Effect: Close burst 3 (enemies in the burst). The target is blinded until the start of its next turn. Also, the globe is destroyed.



Published in Halls of Undermountain, page(s) 25.
