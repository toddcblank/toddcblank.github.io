Barbarian Attack 13Storm of Blades

You lift your weapon again and again, each blow’s impact fueling the next swing.

Encounter        Primal, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 1[W] + Strength modifier damage. Repeat the attack until you miss or until you make three attacks. As soon as an attack misses, this power ends.
