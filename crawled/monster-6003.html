Traits

Half-Remembered Swordplay

Whenever Keljack hits an enemy that is granting combat advantage to him, he also imposes a random effect on that enemy. Roll a d4:

1. The enemy is dazed (save ends).

2. The enemy is immobilized (save ends).

3. The enemy is slowed (save ends).

4. The enemy takes ongoing 5 damage (save ends).

Standard Actions

Longsword (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +13 vs. AC

Hit: 2d8 + 7 damage.

Fumbled Footwork At-Will

Effect: Keljack shifts up to 1d6 + 2 squares away from his starting position and uses longsword at any point during the move.

Triggered Actions

Left-Handed Parry Recharge

Trigger: Keljack is hit by a melee or a ranged attack.

Effect (Immediate Interrupt): Roll a d20. On a 10 or higher, the triggering attack misses.
