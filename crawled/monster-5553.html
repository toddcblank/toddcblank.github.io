Traits

Double Actions

The ettin makes two initiative checks and takes a full turn on each initiative result. The ettin can take two immediate actions per round but only one between one turn and the next.

Standard Actions

Smash At-Will

Attack: Melee 2 (one creature); +13 vs. AC

Hit: 1d12 + 3 damage.

Move Actions

Kick At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +11 vs. Fortitude

Hit: The ettin pushes the target up to 2 squares. The ettin then moves up to half its speed.
