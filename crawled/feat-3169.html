Darkening Mind [Augment]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Shadar-kai, shadow jaunt racial power, any psionic class
Benefit: You can spend 1 power point when you use shadow jaunt to add 2 squares to the distance you would teleport. In addition, you remain insubstantial until the end of your next turn.

Published in Dragon Magazine 389, Dungeon Magazine 182.
