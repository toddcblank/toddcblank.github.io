Ranger Attack 27Beastly Vise

Your attack leaves your foe with no idea how to escape its position between you and your beast.

Encounter        Beast, Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon (beast 1)

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage. If you and your beast companion are both flanking the target, the target is immobilized until the start of your next turn.

  Beast: If your companion is a boar, a cat, a raptor, a spider, or a wolf, the attack deals extra damage equal to your Wisdom modifier.


Published in Martial Power, page(s) 56.
