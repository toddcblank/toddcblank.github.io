Constrictor’s Grasp

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Druid, wild shape power
Benefit: You gain a +2 feat bonus to skill checks made when using the escape action.
Whenever a creature you are grabbing while you are in beast form fails an escape attempt to end the grab, it takes damage equal to the higher of your Constitution modifier or Dexterity modifier.

Published in Dragon Magazine 383, page(s) 38.
