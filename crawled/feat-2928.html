Rose King's Shield

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Avenger or swordmage, proficiency with longsword
Benefit: You gain a +2 feat bonus to Endurance checks.
Once per turn when you hit an enemy with an attack power associated with this feat while using a longsword, you gain 5 temporary hit points. If you ended your turn more than 2 squares away from where you started, you instead gain 10 temporary hit points.


Associated Powers: Booming Blade, Focused Fury

Published in Dragon Magazine 386, page(s) 66.
