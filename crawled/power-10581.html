Rampaging Brute Attack 11Barreling Swing

You rush through your foes, swinging your weapon in sweeping arcs as you arrive in their midst.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Close burst 1

Effect: You charge and make the following attack in place of a melee basic attack. When charging, you can move through enemies’ squares.

  Target: Each enemy in the burst you can see

  Attack: Strength vs. AC

  Hit: 2[W] + Strength modifier damage.

    Weapon: If you’re wielding a two-handed mace or a two-handed hammer, the attack deals extra damage equal to your Constitution modifier.
