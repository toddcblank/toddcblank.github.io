Traits

Curse of Resurrection

When the warrior dies, it rises from the dead 24 hours later with full hit points alongside the rest of the legion.

Recruiting for the Legion

Any living humanoid slain by the warrior has a 50 percent chance of rising 24 hours later as an Accursed Legion warrior alongside the rest of the legion.

Tectuktitlay's Decree

The warrior cannot attack Tectuktitlay, his templars, or the Grim Lasher.

Hit: 2d10 + 10 damage, and the warrior pushes the target up to 3 squares, then shifts up to 3 squares to a square adjacent to the target.

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +18 vs. AC
