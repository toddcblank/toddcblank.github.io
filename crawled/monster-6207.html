Traits

Firetusk Rage

While the boar is bloodied, it gains resist 10 fire. In addition, its melee attacks gain the fire keyword and deal 10 extra fire damage.

Standard Actions

Gore At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +19 vs. AC

Hit: 3d8 + 11 damage.

Firetusk Rush Recharge when first bloodied

Effect: The boar can move up to its speed and enter enemies’ spaces. Each time the boar enters an enemy’s space for the first time during the move, it uses gore against that enemy, and on a hit, the enemy also falls prone.

Triggered Actions

Death Strike Encounter

Trigger: The boar drops to 0 hit points.

Attack (No Action): The boar uses gore.
