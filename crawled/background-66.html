Assassin

I bear the Sigil of Vaurom. I have tasted the Black Lotus Tea and seen the sublime mysteries within Vaurom’s Impossible Crypt. I say this not as a boast, but as a warning.

Type: Campaign
Campaign Setting: Scales of War Adventure Path

You’ve been trained in the thousand arts of death, sent to preemptively kill the powerful. What secret order taught you your trade, and how did they recruit you? What targets have you assassinated, and did any escape your clutches? Do you have a current contract, did you give up the assassin’s life, or are you on the run?
Good Background: Rogue.
    Benefit: You add Arcana and Stealth to your class skill list, and you gain a +1 bonus to Arcana and Stealth checks.

Published in Dragon Magazine 366, page(s) 60.
