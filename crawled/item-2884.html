Power (Thunder) Daily (Free Action)

Use this power when you would push one or more creatures with one of your powers. Instead of pushing those creatures, knock them prone and deal xd6 thunder damage to each one, where x equals the number of squares you would normally push each one.

Level 13 or 18: deal xd8 thunder damage.

Level 23 or 28: deal xd10 thunder damage.
