Encounter        Full Discipline, Implement, Psionic
Standard Action      Melee touch

Target: One creature

Attack: Dexterity vs. Reflex

Hit: 3d10 + Dexterity modifier damage, and you push the target a number of squares equal to your Strength modifier and knock it prone.
