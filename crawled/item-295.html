Ghoul Candle


Category:
Price: 10 gp
Weight: 1/10 lb
Description: Fashioned from fat rendered from a slain ghoul, a ghoul candle sheds light that is invisible to undead. It functions as a normal candle, except that it provides no illumination for undead creatures. Gravediggers use these candles when they must venture into a cemetery at night, in hopes of avoiding the notice of any ghosts or similar creatures that might be about.

Published in Heroes of Shadow, page(s) 159.
