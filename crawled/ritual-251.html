Tension Wheel
Level 4 Uncommon

With a click, the tension wheel unlocks and propels the bolt through your enemy.

Price: 40 gp
Alchemical Item
Prerequisite: You must be holding a crossbow.

Power (Consumable): Minor Action. Place the tension wheel on your crossbow. The next time you make an attack with the crossbow, its normal and long ranges increase by 2 squares and it deals 2 extra damage.
