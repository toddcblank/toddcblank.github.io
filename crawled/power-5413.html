Shaman Utility 22Doorway to the Spirit World

The boundaries between the spiritual and the physical waver as you open a doorway to the spirit world to protect your allies from harm.

Daily        Primal, Zone
Minor Action      Area burst 1 within 10 squares

Effect: The burst creates a zone of primal energy that lasts until the end of the encounter. While within the zone, you and your allies are insubstantial.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 129.
