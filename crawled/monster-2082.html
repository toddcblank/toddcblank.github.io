Warped Grimlock Zombie
Medium aberrant animate (blind, undead)
Level 11 Minion XP 150

Initiative +3        Senses Perception +7; blindsight 5
HP 1; a missed attack never damages a minion.
AC 25; Fortitude 24, Reflex 22, Will 22
Immune disease, gaze, poison
Speed 6

Viscera Grab (standard, at-will)

Reach 2; +16 vs AC; 7 damage, and the target is grabbed (escape ends). The zombie can grab only one target at a time, but the zombie can maintain the grab as long as it remains within 2 squares of the target.

Burrowing Entrails (standard, at-will)

grabbed target only; 10 necrotic damage.

Corpse Seeming

If the zombie is inactive, it appears to be a corpse (DC 18 Insight to see through the ruse).
