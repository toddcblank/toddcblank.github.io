Leaf Runner Sneak [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Rogue, Leaf Runner Student feat
Benefit: If you are attacking with a hand crossbow, a repeating crossbow, a shortbow, a shuriken, or a sling and you have proficiency with that weapon, you gain the following benefit.
Your attacks with powers associated with this feat ignore cover and concealment and take only a -2 penalty to attack rolls against a target that has total concealment or superior cover.
