Cat Burglar

Prerequisite: Rogue

As a master athlete, you become a rogue of a higher caliber who can surprise adversaries with unbelievable moves and amazing feats of physical stamina while remaining true to your roots as a thief or a scoundrel.

CAT BURGLAR PATH FEATURES

    Acrobatic Action (11th level): When you spend an action point to take an extra action, you also gain an extra move action that you must use this turn.
    Body Control (11th level): Whenever you are affected by a pull, a push, or a slide, you can move 1 less square than the effect specifies.
    Athletic Master (16th level): Roll twice whenever you make an Athletics check. Use whichever result you prefer.
