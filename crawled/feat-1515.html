Divine Distraction

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Avenger, Censure of Unity class feature, oath of enmity power
Benefit: When you are adjacent to your oath of enmity target, your allies gain a +1 bonus to damage rolls against that target.
The bonus increases to +2 at 11th level and +3 at 21st level.

Published in Divine Power, page(s) 131.
