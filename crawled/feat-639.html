Improved Darkspiral Aura

Paragon Tier
Prerequisite: 11th level, Warlock, dark pact
Benefit: Your Darkspiral Aura deals 1d10 points of damage per point in the Darkspiral Aura (instead of 1d6).
At 21st level the damage increases to 1d12 per point in the Darkspiral Aura.
