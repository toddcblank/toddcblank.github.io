Trigger: The yagnodemon hits with a melee attack.

Soul Reaper At-Will

Trigger: The yagnodemon reduces an enemy to 0 hit points or fewer.

Effect (No Action): The yagnodemon gains 10 temporary hit points.

Variable Resistance 2/Encounter

Trigger: The yagnodemon takes acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder damage.

Effect (Free Action): The yagnodemon gains resist 15 to the triggering damage type until the end of the encounter or until it uses variable resistance again.
