Traits

Incite Frenzy Aura 5

While in the aura, allies gain a +5 bonus to damage rolls against bloodied enemies.

Brutal Reach

Orwantz can make opportunity attacks against creatures within 3 squares of him. If Orwantz hits with an opportunity attack, the target falls prone.

Standard Actions

Slam At-Will

Attack: Melee 3 (one creature); +32 vs. AC

Hit: 3d12 + 25 damage.

Double Attack At-Will

Effect: Orwantz uses slam twice.

Goring Charge At-Will

Effect: Orwantz charges and makes the following attack in place of a melee basic attack.

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +32 vs. AC

Hit: 7d10 + 25 damage, Orwantz pushes the target up to 3 squares, and the target falls prone.

Effect: Orwantz can then shift up to 3 squares and use slam.

Dictum of Graz'zt (charm) Encounter

Attack: Ranged 5 (one or two creatures); +30 vs. Will

Hit: The target is dominated (save ends).

Miss: The target is dazed (save ends).

Triggered Actions

Variable Resistance 2/Encounter

Trigger: Orwantz takes acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder damage.

Effect (Free Action): Orwantz gains resist 30 to the triggering damage type until the end of the encounter or until he uses variable resistance again.
