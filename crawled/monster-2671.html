Close burst 1; four abyssal ghoul hungerers appear in squares Hertrud designates in the burst area. If a ghoul appears in an occupied space, that space’s occupant is pushed 2 squares.

Until the end of Hertrud’s next turn, each creature that hits her with a melee attack takes 3d8 + 2 necrotic damage.
