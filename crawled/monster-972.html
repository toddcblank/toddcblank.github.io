Aimed Shot

Zark gains a +2 bonus to attack rolls and deals an extra 1d6 damage with ranged attacks against creatures that don’t have cover.

Stand Your Ground (standard; encounter)

When an effect forces Zark to move—through a push, a pull, or a slide—he moves 1 square less than the effect specifies. When an attack would knock Zark prone, the dwarf gains an immediate saving throw to avoid falling prone.
