Ardent Attack 25Outpouring of Grief

You channel psionic power through your foe, letting you turn one ally’s pain into another’s healing.

Daily        Healing, Psionic, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Charisma vs. Fortitude

Hit: 5[W] + Charisma modifier damage.

Effect: Until the target drops to 0 hit points, when an ally becomes bloodied by an attack while you are within 5 squares of the target, another ally adjacent to you or the target can spend a healing surge.


Published in Psionic Power, page(s) 20.
