Karruuk's CircletLevel 10 Uncommon

This mithral and gold headband enhances your knowledge and forges an unseen link between the scattered fragments of the Ashen Crown.

Head Slot        5,000 gp

Property

You gain a +2 item bonus to Arcana checks, History checks, and Religion checks.

Power Daily

You can use a Detect Object effect (as the ritual; PH 303) to know the distance and direction to any one fragment of the Ashen Crown. An Arcana check determines the range of the effect.

Arcana CheckResult Range
9 or lower2 miles
10-195 miles
20-2915 miles
30-3930 miles
40 or higher60 miles

If you do not use this power to locate a particular Crown fragment, or the closest fragment is out of range, you sense the general direction to the closest fragment.



Published in Seekers of the Ashen Crown, page(s) 24.
