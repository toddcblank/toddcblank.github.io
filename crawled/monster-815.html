+9 vs AC; 1d6+3 damage.

Crossbow (standard, at-will) Weapon

Combat Advantage

When Balgron has combat advantage, his ranged attacks deal +2d6 damage.

Crippling Strike (standard; encounter)

Enemy struck by melee attack is slowed (speed becomes 2) and takes a -2 penalty on opportunity attacks until the end of Balgron's next turn.

Goblin Tactics (immediate reaction; at-will)

Balgron can shift 1 square.

Shifty (minor; at-will)

Balgron can shift 1 square as a minor action.
