Warlord Attack 17Disabling Missile

Your carefully aimed shot leaves a foe unable to concentrate on defending itself.

Encounter        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Ranged weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + Strength modifier damage, and each ally adjacent to the target can shift 1 square as a free action. Until the end of your next turn, the target provokes opportunity attacks from your allies whenever it attacks.

  Skirmishing Presence: You and your allies gain a power bonus to melee damage rolls against the target equal to your Intelligence modifier or Wisdom modifier. The power bonus lasts until the end of your next turn.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 91.
