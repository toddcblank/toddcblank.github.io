Wild Talent CantripPsionic Image

You plant the image of an object in the minds of nearby creatures.

At-Will      Illusion, Psionic
Minor Action      Close burst 5

Target: One or more creatures in the burst

Effect: Each target sees the image of a Small or smaller object in your hand or in a square in the burst. The image lasts until the end of your next turn.


Published in Dark Sun Campaign Setting, page(s) 81.
