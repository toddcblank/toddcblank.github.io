Singularity GrenadeLevel 11 Rare

The size of a large fruit, this device is a polished white color, as if made of ivory. In the center is a hole made of pure blackness that seems to spin.

Consumable        350 gp

Attack Power (Cold, Thunder) Consumable (Standard Action)

Attack: Area burst 2d3 - 1 within 10 (creatures in the burst); +14 vs. Fortitude

Malfunction: If you roll a 1 on one or more of the attack rolls, you teleport to the center of the burst, or the nearest unoccupied square, and the attack hits you.

Hit: Roll 1d4. Then roll that many d4s. Then roll that many d4s and total the result. Each target takes cold and thunder damage equal to the result.

Miss: Half damage.

Special: If you score a critical hit with this attack, no damage dice are maximized.



Published in Dragon Magazine 410.
