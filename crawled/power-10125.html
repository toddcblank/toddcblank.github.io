Warlord Attack 5Rousing Call

Fallen comrades cannot help but answer your call because your battle cry stirs the most melancholy hearts.

Daily        Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: Each unconscious or dying ally within 5 squares of you can make a saving throw and each prone ally within 5 squares of you can take the stand up action as a free action.


Published in Dragon Magazine 381, page(s) 32.
