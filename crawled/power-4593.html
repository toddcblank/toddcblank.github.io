Warlord Utility 22Quickening Order

You aim to win the fight before your foes can respond.

Daily        Martial
Free Action      Close burst 10

Trigger: You and your allies make initiative checks at the beginning of an encounter

Target: You and each ally in the burst

Effect: Each target gains a power bonus to the initiative check equal to your Intelligence modifier, and during each target’s first turn, he or she gains a bonus to damage rolls equal to your Intelligence modifier.


Update (4/9/2010)
In the Action Type entry, replace “No Action” with “Free Action.” In the item’s Effect entry, add “power” before each occurrence of bonus. In addition, delete “attack rolls and.”
