swarm

A swarm is composed of multiple creatures but functions as a single creature. A swarm can occupy the same space as another creature, and an enemy can enter a swarmâ€™s space, which is difficult terrain. A swarm cannot be pulled, pushed, or slid by melee or ranged attacks.
    A swarm can squeeze through any opening large enough for even one of its constituent creatures. For example, a swarm of bats can squeeze through an opening large enough for one of the bats to squeeze through.
