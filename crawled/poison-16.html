Mindscorch
Level 15 Poison

Brewed from brain matter harvested from mind flayers, this potent toxin shreds the mind.

Poison        6250 gp
Attack: +18 vs. Will; ongoing 10 poison damage and -2 penalty to close or area attack rolls (save ends).
First Failed Saving Throw: The target is dazed (save ends).

Published in Dragon Magazine 367.
