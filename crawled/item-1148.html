Dwarven BootsLevel 12 Uncommon

These iron boots keep you steady in the face of forceful assaults.

Feet Slot        13,000 gp

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

Use this power when an attack would knock you prone or pull, push, slide, or immobilize you. The attacker rerolls the attack, using the second result even if it's lower.
