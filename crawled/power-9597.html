Barbarian Utility 22Blood Seeker's Pursuit

You are drawn to foes closer to death, and their wounds fortify you.

Encounter        Primal
Move Action      Personal

Requirement: No enemies are adjacent to you.

Effect: You shift your speed. You gain 1d8 + your Constitution modifier temporary hit points, plus 1d8 temporary hit points for each bloodied enemy adjacent to you at the end of the shift.


Published in Primal Power, page(s) 20.
