Orb of Stored EnergyLevel 5+ Uncommon

This glittering sphere stores psionic energy that you can unleash when your own power has waned.

Lvl 10+25,000 gp Lvl 25+5625,000 gp
Lvl 15+325,000 gp Lvl 30+63,125,000 gp

Implement: Orb

Enhancement Bonus: attack rolls and damage rolls

Critical: +1d6 damage per plus

Power Daily (Free Action)

Expend 1 or 2 power points and place them in the orb. They remain there until you use them or until you take an extended rest.

Power Daily (Free Action)

Requirement: You must have at least 1 power point stored inside the orb.

Trigger: You make an attack using an unaugmented attack power.

Effect: You can augment the triggering power by spending 1 power point from the orb, instead of using your own power points to augment the power.

Level 25 or 30: You can augment the triggering attack by spending 1 or 2 power points from the orb.
