Domino of OlladraLevel 10+ Uncommon

The numbers on this white and gray domino's face change every time you use a prayer, emphasizing your deity's capricious nature.

Lvl 20+4125,000 gp

Implement: Holy symbol

Enhancement Bonus: attack rolls and damage rolls

Critical: +1d6 damage per plus

Requirement: You must worship olladra or the sovereign host to use this holy symbol.

Power Daily (Immediate Reaction)

Trigger: An ally within your line of sight hits with an attack.

Effect: You roll a d20. If your roll is higher than the d20 roll of the triggering ally's attack, that attack scores a critical hit. If your roll is lower than the d20 roll of the triggering ally's attack, you take a -2 penalty to attack rolls until the end of your next turn.
