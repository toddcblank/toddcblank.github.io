Gophodros, Spellwarping Otyugh
Large natural beast
Level 9 Elite Soldier XP 800

Initiative +6        Senses Perception +12; Darkvision
Otyugh Stench aura 1; each living enemy in the aura takes a –2 penalty to attack rolls.
HP 196; Bloodied 98
AC 23; Fortitude 24, Reflex 18, Will 21
Immune disease
Saving Throws +2
Speed 5, climb 3
Action Points 1

Tentacle (standard, at-will)

Reach 3; +15 vs AC; 1d8+6 damage, and the target is pulled 2 squares and grabbed.

Eldritch Bite (standard, at-will) Disease

Reach 2; +13 vs Reflex; 1d6+4 damage, and ongoing 5 damage (save ends). In addition, the target is exposed to filth fever (see Monster Manual, page 211).

Eldritch Flurry (standard, at-will)

Gophodros makes two melee basic attacks

Gnaw (standard, at-will)

Reach 2; targets a creature grabbed by Gophodros; +15 vs AC; 2d10+6 damage, and ongoing 10 damage (save ends). In addition, the target is exposed to filth fever (see Monster Manual, page 211).

Spell Warped

At the start of Gophodros’s turn, it chooses one of the following:
1. Regain 10 hit points.
2. Gain a +2 bonus to all defenses until the end of its next turn.
3. Gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls until the end of its next turn.
4. Gain resist 20 to acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder damage.
