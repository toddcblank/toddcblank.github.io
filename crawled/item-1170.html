Ebon GauntletsLevel 13 Common

The inner palms of these black gauntlets are covered with small metal studs. Touching the studs causes a faint sense of nausea to course through you.

Hands Slot        17,000 gp

Property

When a creature you have grabbed fails an escape attempt against your grab, that creature takes 5 necrotic damage, and you regain 5 hit points.
