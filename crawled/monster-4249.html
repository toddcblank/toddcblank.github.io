Level 5 Skirmisher XP 200

Initiative +8        Senses Perception +9
HP 62; Bloodied 31
AC 19; Fortitude 16, Reflex 18, Will 17
Speed 6

Short Sword (standard, at-will) Weapon

The stalker’s attack deals 1d6 extra damage to any target granting combat advantage to it.

Alignment Unaligned        Languages Common

Equipment: crossbow , crossbow bolts (20) , leather armor , short sword .

Description: Justice stalkers are the scouts, spies, and killers of the Eye of Justice. Most are fully aware of the ignoble brutality of the Eye and couldn’t care less. If the PCs are clearly good hearted, the Seers send justice stalkers to deal with them.
