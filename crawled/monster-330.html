Magma Strider
Large elemental magical beast (earth, fire)
Level 10 Skirmisher XP 500

Initiative +12        Senses Perception +7
Blazing Heat (Fire) aura 2; enemies in the aura at the start of their turns take 5 fire damage
HP 105; Bloodied 52
AC 24; Fortitude 20, Reflex 22, Will 19
Immune petrification; Resist 10 fire; Vulnerable cold (a magma strider that takes cold damage is slowed until the end of its next turn)
Speed 6, climb 6

Bite (standard, at-will) Fire

Reach 2; +15 vs AC; 1d6+5 damage, and ongoing 5 fire damage (save ends).

Burn Across the Battlefield (free, when the magma strider hits with its bite attack, recharge )

The magma strider can charge another target up to 6 squares away and make another bite attack.

Burning Mobility Fire

Any creature that makes a opportunity attack against the magma strider takes ongoing 5 fire damage (save ends).

Alignment Unaligned        Languages Primordial
Skills Endurance +13
Str 16 (+8)      Dex 21 (+10)      Wis 14 (+7)
Con 17 (+8)      Int 2 (+1)      Cha 10 (+5)

Description: Magma striders are relentless predators that are willing to pursue victims for miles, wearing down their prey through sheer persistence.
