Disciple of Light

Prerequisite: Wisdom 13
Benefit: Whenever you spend a healing surge while bloodied, each ally within 5 squares of you gains 3 temporary hit points. These temporary hit points increase to 5 at 11th level and 8 at 21st level.

Published in Heroes of the Fallen Lands, page(s) 312, Heroes of the Forgotten Kingdoms, page(s) 313.
