Champion of the Labyrinth Attack 11Dead End Strike

You smite your foe with an attack and are prepared to spring if it should run.

Encounter        Weapon
Standard Action      Melee or Ranged weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Your highest ability vs. AC

Hit: 2[W] + your highest ability modifier damage. In addition, if the target moves before the start of your next turn, you can shift your speed to a square adjacent to the target as an opportunity action. If you do, the target takes 5 damage and ends its movement.


Published in Dragon Magazine 385, page(s) 22.
