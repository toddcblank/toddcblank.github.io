Will-o'-Wisp
Small fey magical beast
Level 10 Lurker XP 500

HP 57; Bloodied 28Initiative +15
AC 24, Fortitude 18, Reflex 24, Will 22Perception+11
Speed 0, fly 6 (hover; altitude limit 2)

Traits

Fey Light

A will-o’-wisp can give off light as a torch or extinguish this light as a free action. Fey light must be on for the will-o’-wisp to make any attack. When giving off no light, the will-o’-wisp is invisible.

Insubstantial

The will-o’-wisp takes half damage from all attacks, except those that deal force damage.

Standard Actions

Glimmer Wisp (radiant) At-Will

Attack: Melee 2 (one creature); +13 vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d6 + 11 radiant damage.

Spirit Drain (healing, psychic) Encounter

Attack: Melee 3 (one bloodied living creature); +13 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d8 + 9 psychic damage, and the target is weakened (save ends). Additionally, the will-o’-wisp regains 14 hit points.

Luring Glow At-Will

Attack: Close burst 20 (one creature in the burst that can see); +13 vs. Will

Hit: The will-o’-wisp pulls the target up to 3 squares, and the target is dazed (save ends).

Triggered Actions

Blink Out (teleportation) At-Will

Trigger: An attack misses the will-o’-wisp.

Effect (Immediate Interrupt): The will-o’-wisp extinguishes its fey light and teleports up to 5 squares.
