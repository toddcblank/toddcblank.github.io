Cloud Form (standard, at-will) Polymorph, Zone

Close burst 1; the djinn cloudstalker disperses into a billowing cloud. The burst creates a cloudy zone until the cloudstalker uses a minor action to end the effect or is forced to resume humanoid form. While in cloud form, the cloudstalker cannot be attacked and can use only biting wind and turbulence. It can fly at half speed as a move action, squeeze as though it were a Tiny creature, and move through enemy-occupied spaces.
