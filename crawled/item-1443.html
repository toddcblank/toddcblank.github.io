Gloves of the Wandering StarLevel 19 Uncommon

The magic of these supple gloves sends forth a burst of starlight to blind your foes.

Hands Slot        105,000 gp

Property

The range of your ranged attack powers increases by 2 squares.

Power (Implement, Radiant) Daily (Free Action)

Trigger: You hit or miss an enemy with a ranged attack.

Effect: Make a secondary attack: Area burst 1 centered on that enemy; Charisma + 4 vs. Fortitude; targets enemies; on a hit, the attack deals 2d8 + Charisma modifier radiant damage, and the target is blinded until the start of your next turn.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 117.
