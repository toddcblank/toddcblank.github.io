Trample (standard, at-will)

The magebred destrier can move up to its speed and enter enemies’ spaces. This movement provokes opportunity attacks, and the destrier must end its move in an unoccupied space. When it enters an enemy’s space, the destrier makes a trample attack; +6 vs Any; 1d8+6 damage, and the target is knocked prone.

Charger (while mounted by a friendly rider of 5th level or higher) Mount

When a destrier’s rider charges, the rider gains a +6 bonus to damage rolls.

Bonded Mount ( when a friendly rider of 5th level or higher spends a healing surge; encounter) Mount

The magebred destrier gains temporary hit points equal to the rider’s healing surge value.
