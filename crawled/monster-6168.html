Bloodthirsty Shadow (cold, necrotic) Aura 1

When a bloodied enemy in the aura is hit by an attack, that enemy takes 5 extra cold and necrotic damage from the attack.

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +13 vs. AC

Whirling Flail At-Will

Effect: Arturas uses dread flail twice.

Shadow Contamination (cold, fear, implement, necrotic) Recharge

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +11 vs. Will

Hit: 1d10 + 8 cold and necrotic damage, ongoing 10 cold and necrotic damage (save ends), and Arturas pushes the target up to 3 squares. While the target is taking the ongoing damage, any of its allies adjacent to it at the start of its turn take 5 cold and necrotic damage.
