Sweeping Tentacle
Medium aberrant beast (blind)
Level 12 Minion Brute XP 175

HP 1; a missed attack never damages a minion.Initiative +12

Traits

Unhindered

When the sweeping tentacle moves, it pulls with it any creature grabbed by it. In addition, the creature remains grabbed, and the tentacle does not provoke an opportunity attack from the grabbed creature.

Standard Actions

Seize At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +17 vs. AC

Hit: 13 damage, and the tentacle grabs the target.

Suffocating Grab At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature grabbed by the tentacle); +15 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 13 damage, and the target falls prone.
