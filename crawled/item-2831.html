Tattoo of the Penitent MartyrLevel 11 Uncommon

This tattoo depicts a pair of hands held together in prayer and bound with blood-red cord.

Wondrous Item        9,000 gp

Property

Once while using a daily attack power, when you miss with an attack roll, you can take damage equal twice the number of healing surges you have remaining. If you do so, you reroll the missed attack roll.



Published in Dungeon Magazine 177, page(s) 71.
