Cleric Utility 10Phantom Vigor

Tendrils of shadow unfurl around you to wrap your allies, drawing off their health to grant superior strength and speed.

Daily        Divine, Shadow
Standard Action      Close burst 5

Target: You and each ally in the burst

Effect: Each target loses a healing surge and gains temporary hit points equal to his or her healing surge value. In addition, until the end of the encounter, each target gains a +2 power bonus to speed and a +5 power bonus to Athletics checks and Acrobatics checks.


Published in Heroes of Shadow, page(s) 84.
