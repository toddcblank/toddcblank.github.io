Gauntlets of Ogre PowerLevel 5 Rare

These oversized armored gloves increase your strength and can be activated to increase your damage.

Hands Slot        1,000 gp

Property

You gain a +1 item bonus to Athletics checks and Strength ability checks (but not Strength attacks).

Power Daily (Minor Action)

You gain a +2 power bonus to melee damage rolls until the end of the encounter.
