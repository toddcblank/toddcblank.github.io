Vaprak, The Destroyer

Type: Exarch
Alignment: Chaotic Evil
Gender: Male
Sphere: Frenzy
Dominion: Nishrek
Priests: Shamans
Adjective: Vapran

The rapacious god of the ogres, Vaprak is a god of combat, destruction, aggression, frenzy, and greed.
