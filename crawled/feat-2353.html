Leaf Runner Pathfinder [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Warlord, Leaf Runner Student feat
Benefit: If you are attacking with a hand crossbow, a repeating crossbow, a shortbow, a shuriken, or a sling and you have proficiency with that weapon, you gain the following two benefits.
Your attacks with powers associated with this feat ignore cover and concealment.
You can use a power associated with this feat as a ranged power instead of a melee power.


Associated Powers: Battle On, Inspiring War Cry, Rabbits and Wolves, Surprise Attack, Uplifting Assault, Withdrawal Gambit

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 119.
