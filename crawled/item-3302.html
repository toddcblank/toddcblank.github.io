Quaal's Feather PigeonLevel 16 Uncommon

This feather turns into a messenger pigeon that awaits your instruction.

Consumable        1,800 gp

Utility Power Consumable (Minor Action)

Effect: You produce a small bird that can travel 50 miles a day to unerringly reach any location or individual you name, carrying with it a small written message. The bird disappears after delivering its message.



Published in Mordenkainen's Magnificent Emporium, page(s) 100.
