True Sending

Your mental voice can reach anywhere and everywhere, across planar boundaries or within them.

Component Cost: 4,000 gp
Market Price: 25,000 gp
Key Skill: Arcana (no check)Level: 20
Category: Exploration
Time: 30 minutes
Duration: Instantaneous

You convey a mental message of up to 50 words to a person you know. The message reaches the intended destination despite any amount of distance between you and the target, including planar boundaries. He or she receives the message mentally and can respond likewise.

Published in Dragon Magazine 366, page(s) 32.
