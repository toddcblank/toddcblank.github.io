Flash FlowerLevel 11+ Uncommon

This flower, which is sometimes found near lightning-struck ground, contains electrical magic.

Lvl 11350 gp Lvl 219,000 gp

Consumable: Reagent

Power Consumable (Free Action)

You expend this reagent when you hit with a lightning power of up to 7th level. You choose one target hit by the attack. It's also blinded until the end of your next turn. This reagent has no effect if the power already blinds the target.

Level 21: Power up to 17th level.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 88.
