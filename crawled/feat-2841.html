Flamespeed Shifting

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Razorclaw shifter, razorclaw shifting racial power, you have a spellscar
Benefit: While under the effect of razorclaw shifting, you can shift 2 squares as a move action. If you have the Student of the Plague feat, whenever you shift while under the effect razorclaw shifting, your attacks deal 2 extra fire damage until the end of your turn.

Published in Dragon Magazine 385, page(s) 24.
