Arcane Repositioning

Prerequisite: Eladrin, artificer
Benefit: On your turn, you can use a free action to expend your fey step racial power to teleport one of your conjurations or a creature that you summoned rather than yourself. If you do so, select one conjuration or summoned creature within 5 of squares of you and teleport it 5 squares. You cannot teleport a conjuration larger than 1 square or a summoned creature that is size Large or larger.

Published in Dragon Magazine 390.
