Sand Trap Recharge when all this power’s sand traps have ended

Effect: The desert talker creates sand traps in three unoccupied squares within 10 squares of it. The sand traps must be created on a solid surface. Until the end of the encounter, any enemy that enters a sand trap’s square is restrained until the end of its next turn, and that sand trap disappears.
