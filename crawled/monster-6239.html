White Pudding
Large natural beast (blind, cold, ooze)
Level 16 Elite Brute XP 2800

HP 388; Bloodied 194Initiative +12
AC 28, Fortitude 30, Reflex 27, Will 26Perception+8
Speed 4, climb 3Blindsight 10
Immune blinded, gaze; Resist 15 acid, 15 cold; Vulnerable 10 fire
Saving Throws +2; Action Points 1

Traits

Icy Camouflage

If the pudding is in icy or snowy surroundings, until the pudding takes an action, a creature must succeed on a DC 31 Perception check to recognize that the pudding is a creature. In such surroundings, the pudding gains a +5 bonus to Stealth checks.

Mercurial Body

The pudding ignores difficult terrain and cannot be knocked prone.

Ooze

While squeezing, the pudding moves at full speed rather than half speed, it doesn’t take the -5 penalty to attack rolls, and it doesn’t grant combat advantage for squeezing.

Standard Actions

Slam (cold) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +19 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d10 + 13 cold damage, and the target is slowed (save ends).

Engulf (acid, cold) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +19 vs. Fortitude

Hit: 2d10 + 13 acid and cold damage, and the pudding grabs the target (escape DC 22).

Digest (acid, cold) At-Will

Effect: Melee 1 (one creature grabbed by the pudding); 3d10 + 13 acid and cold damage.

Triggered Actions

Split At-Will

Trigger: An enemy hits the pudding with a weapon attack.

Effect (No Action): A white pudding spawn appears in a square adjacent to the pudding or in the nearest unoccupied square.
