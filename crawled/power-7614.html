Vadalis Griffonmaster Attack 11Griffon's Pull

Your griffon reaches out with its beak and pulls your enemies close. You then seize the opportunity to attack.

Encounter        Beast, Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon (beast 2)

Primary Target: One creature

Primary Attack: Beast’s attack bonus vs. Reflex

Hit: 1[B] + beast’s Strength modifier damage, and you slide the target 3 squares into a space adjacent to your Vadalis-bred griffon beast companion. Make a secondary attack that is a melee weapon attack.

  Secondary Target: One creature that was a primary target

  Secondary Attack: Strength vs. AC. You have combat advantage against the secondary target.

  Hit: 1[W] + Strength modifier damage.
Level 21: 2[W] + Strength modifier damage.


Published in Eberron Player's Guide, page(s) 79.
