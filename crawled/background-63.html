Last of the Breed

Everyone says that the Resolute Two Hundred are no more. That’s not true—not as long I draw breath, anyway.

Type: Campaign
Campaign Setting: Scales of War Adventure Path

You were part of an elite order of roving guardians—but they’re all gone and only you remain. What caused the order to die out? How did you escape that fate? Will you try to rejuvenate the order, or are you resigned to it passing into history?
Good Background: Ranger.
    Benefit: You add Endurance and Perception to your class skill list, and you gain a +1 bonus to Endurance and Perception checks.

Published in Dragon Magazine 366, page(s) 59.
