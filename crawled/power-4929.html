Barbarian Attack 25Circle of Blood Rage

You spin, hacking at your enemies. The image of the circle of blood used in your initiation rites grips your mind, empowering your later attacks.

Daily        Primal, Rage, Weapon
Standard Action      Close burst 1

Target: Each creature in the burst

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 4[W] + Strength modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: You enter the rage of the circle of blood. Until the rage ends, you gain a power bonus to attack rolls with barbarian at-will attack powers and rage strike equal to the number of creatures you targeted with this power.


Published in Primal Power, page(s) 22.
