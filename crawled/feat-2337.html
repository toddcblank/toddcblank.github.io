Harrowing Swarm Scout [Greater Style]

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Ranger, Harrowing Swarm Student feat
Benefit: When you are attacking with a bow or a crossbow and you have proficiency with that weapon, you gain the following two benefits.
You do not provoke an opportunity attack from the target of your attack.
When you use a power associated with this feat, you can use your bow or crossbow instead of a thrown weapon. If you do so, you use Dexterity instead of Strength for attack rolls and damage rolls.


Associated Powers: Quick Throw, Ricochet Throw, Skewering Shot, Surprising Throw, Upending Throw

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 116.
