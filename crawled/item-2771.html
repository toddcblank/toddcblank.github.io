Symbol of Divine ReachLevel 3+ Uncommon

This symbol lets you extend the reach of your retribution against enemies of your deity.

Lvl 3+1680 gp Lvl 18+485,000 gp
Lvl 8+23,400 gp Lvl 23+5425,000 gp
Lvl 13+317,000 gp Lvl 28+62,125,000 gp

Implement: Holy symbol

Enhancement Bonus: attack rolls and damage rolls

Critical: +1d6 damage per plus, +1d10 damage per plus with ranged and area attacks.

Property

When using this holy symbol to deliver a ranged or area prayer, add the symbol’s enhancement bonus to the range. For example, a +3 symbol of divine reach would increase “area burst 5 within 10 squares” to “area burst 5 within 13 squares.”
