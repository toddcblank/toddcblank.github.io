Cleric Attack 1Priest's Shield

You utter a minor defensive prayer to protect a comrade as you attack with your weapon.

At-Will        Divine, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 1[W] + Strength modifier damage, and you and one ally adjacent to you gains a +1 power bonus to AC until the end of your next turn.
    Level 21: 2[W] + Strength modifier damage.
