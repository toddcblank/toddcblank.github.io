Mask of the MatriarchLevel 18 Uncommon

An obsidian mask, it is carved to resemble the features of a beautiful drow woman.

Head Slot        85,000 gp

Property

You gain a +2 item bonus to Intimidate checks.

Power (Fear) Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

This power triggers when you are the target of a melee attack. You cancel the attack and the target must move its speed away from you, provoking opportunity attacks as normal.



Published in P2 Demon Queen Enclave, page(s) 24.
