Heir of Siberys Utility 26Siberys Mark of Passage

You open a passage that transcends the limits of space, inviting your allies to follow you through it.

Daily        Teleportation
Move Action      Personal

Effect: You teleport 20 squares, and each ally adjacent to your starting position can teleport to a square adjacent to your new location. Neither you nor a teleporting ally needs to see your destination.
