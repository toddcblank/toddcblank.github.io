Swordmage Attack 25Icy Shackles

As you drive your blade home, glowing frost courses through your foe, encircling its limbs and anchoring it to the ground.

Daily        Arcane, Cold, Reliable, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Target: One creature

Attack: Intelligence vs. AC

Hit: 4[W] + Intelligence modifier damage, and ongoing 10 cold damage (save ends). Each time the target takes a move action before it has saved against the ongoing cold damage, the target takes extra cold damage equal to your Constitution modifier.


Published in Forgotten Realms Player's Guide, page(s) 33.
