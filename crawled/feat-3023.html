Astral Elixir

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Deva, artificer, shielding elixir power
Benefit: When you use shielding elixir, the target gains radiant resistance in addition to the resistance the power normally grants. The radiant resistance is equal to the resistance ordinarily granted by the power and has the same duration.
    When making a saving throw, the target can end the shielding elixir effect as a free action to add 1d6 to the saving throw result instead of gaining the normal benefit for ending the effect.

Published in Dragon Magazine 387, page(s) 31.
