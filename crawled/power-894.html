Cleric Attack 3Daunting Light

A burning column of light engulfs your foe. Its brilliance burns your enemy and hinders its defenses for a short time.

Encounter        Divine, Implement, Radiant
Standard Action      Ranged 10

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d10 + Wisdom modifier radiant damage.

Effect: One ally you can see gains combat advantage against the target until the end of your next turn.


Published in Player's Handbook, page(s) 65, Class Compendium.
