Initiative +13        Senses Perception +16

AC 28; Fortitude 24, Reflex 26, Will 26

+19 vs AC; 2d8+4 damage.

Elemental Bolts (standard, at-will)

Ranged 10; +17 vs Reflex; 4d8 acid, cold, fire, or lightning damage (the mindmage chooses the damage type for each attack).

Concussion Orb (standard, encounter)

Area burst 2 within 10; +17 vs Fortitude; 1d10+4 damage, and the target is knocked prone.

Accurate Mind

The githzerai mindmage’s ranged attacks ignore cover and concealment (but not superior cover or total concealment).

Iron Mind (immediate interrupt, when hit by an attack; encounter)

The githzerai mindmage gains a +2 bonus to all defenses until the end of its next turn.
