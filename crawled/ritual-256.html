Conceal Dragon mark

Before your eyes, the complex marking fades from view.

Component Cost: 10 gp
Market Price: 50 gp
Key Skill: ArcanaLevel: 1
Category: Deception
Time: 10 minutes
Duration: Until broken

You conceal a dragon mark on yourself or on an adjacent willing target. Creatures that examine the area where the mark is hidden are entitled to a Perception check against your Arcana check result to notice the deception. When you use any powers or effects related to the dragon mark, the effect is broken and the ritual ends.

Published in Eberron Player's Guide, page(s) 116.
