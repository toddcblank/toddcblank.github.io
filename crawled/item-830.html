Cannith GogglesLevel 11 Uncommon

These goggles enhance your vision and can be empowered by magic to improve your vision even more.

Head Slot        9,000 gp

Property

You gain a +2 item bonus to Perception checks.

Power Daily (Minor Action)

Expend an arcane encounter attack power or daily attack power. You gain darkvision until the end of the encounter or for 5 minutes if you expended an encounter power or until the end of your next extended rest if you expended a daily power.



Published in Dragon Magazine 365, page(s) 15.
