Snow Tiger Attack 20Slashing Frenzy

You dive into the middle of your foes, slashing wildly with your blades. The sheer speed and ferocity of your attack terrifies enemies that survive it.

Daily        Fear, Martial, Weapon
Standard Action      Close burst 2

Requirement: You must be wielding two light blades.

Target: Each enemy in the burst you can see

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: Until the end of your next turn, each target you hit takes a penalty to attack rolls equal to the number of targets you hit with this power.


Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 53.
