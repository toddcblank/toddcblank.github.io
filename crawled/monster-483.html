Firelasher
Large elemental magical beast (air, fire)
Level 11 Skirmisher XP 600

Initiative +12        Senses Perception +5
HP 108; Bloodied 54
AC 25; Fortitude 21, Reflex 25, Will 20
Immune disease, poison; Resist 25 fire
Speed fly 8 (hover)

Fire Lash (standard, at-will) Fire

Reach 2; +14 vs Reflex; 2d8+5 fire damage.

Wildfire Cyclone (standard, recharge ) Fire

Close burst 2; +14 vs Reflex; 2d6+5 fire damage, and the target is pushed 1 square and knocked prone. Miss: Half damage, and the target is neither pushed nor knocked prone.

Whirlwind Dash (standard; recharge ) Fire

The firelasher can move up to twice its speed. It can move through spaces occupied by other creatures without provoking opportunity attacks. It must end its move in an unoccupied space. Any creature whose space the firelasher enters takes 10 fire damage.

Mutable Shape

The firelasher can squeeze through spaces as though it were a Medium creature.

Alignment Unaligned        Languages Primordial
Str 11 (+5)      Dex 21 (+10)      Wis 11 (+5)
Con 12 (+6)      Int 7 (+3)      Cha 8 (+4)

Description: Air and fire combine to create a raging creature that swirls across the blasted landscapes of the Elemental Chaos in search of things to destroy.
