Fire Bat
Medium elemental beast (fire)
Level 5 Skirmisher XP 200

Initiative +8        Senses Perception +8
HP 60; Bloodied 30
AC 20; Fortitude 15, Reflex 20, Will 13
Resist 10 fire
Speed 2 (clumsy), Fly 8

Fiery Touch (standard, at-will) Fire

+6 vs Reflex; 1d6+4 fire damage, and ongoing 5 fire damage (save ends).

Fiery Swoop (standard, at-will) Fire

The fire bat shifts up to 4 squares and can move through occupied squares as it moves. It makes a melee basic attack against any creature whose space it enters. The fire bat cannot attack a target more than once in this fashion, and it must end its movement in an unoccupied square
