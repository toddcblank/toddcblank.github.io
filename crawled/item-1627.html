Inquisitor's HelmLevel 16 Uncommon

Your mind can access the secrets of another while you wear this cuir-bouilli skullcap.

Head Slot        45,000 gp

Power Daily (Standard Action)

Make an attack: Ranged 10; Intelligence + 4, Wisdom + 4, or Charisma + 4 vs. Will; on a hit, you pry the answer to one question from the target's mind. If the target doesn't know the answer to the question, you get no answer but the power is still spent.
