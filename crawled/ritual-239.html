Clear-Path Mist

Component Cost: see below
Market Price: 375 gp
Key Skill: Arcana or Nature (no check)Level: 8
Category: Other
Time: 30 minutes

Ordinary undergrowth cannot stand against this concoction—a mere dusting causes plants to wither and die.

Clear-Path Mist
Level 8 Uncommon

The mist clears out the underbrush, making your path easier to travel.

Price: 125 gp
Alchemical Item

Power (Consumable • Poison): Standard Action. Make an attack: Close blast 3; targets plants; +11 vs. Reflex; 1d4 poison damage, and ongoing 5 poison damage (save ends). In addition, you remove any difficult terrain created by flora such as foliage or undergrowth within the area of the attack.
