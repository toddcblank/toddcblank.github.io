Lidded-Eye Obelisk
Trap
Level 10 Blaster
XP 500

A dull granite obelisk seems to watch over the clearing, yet the iron eye that sits atop the pinnacle is tightly shut.

Trap: The magic of the obelisk attempts to put all nearby mortals to sleep.Perception     DC 16: A number of spidery runes are scrawled around the top of the obelisk.Arcana     DC 21: An Arcana check to detect magic reveals that the obelisk is a device that casts sleep magic. The character also realizes that forcing open the eye deactivates the trap.Initiative +2Trigger       When a living creature examines the campsite at location 3, or is within 2 squares of the obelisk, the obelisk activates and rolls initiative.
