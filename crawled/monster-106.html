Whirlwind Charge

When a hellsword charges an enemy, it makes a greatsword attack against each enemy within its reach at the end of its charge.

Triumphant Surge

The cambion hellsword gains 5 temporary hit points each time it bloodies an enemy or reduces an enemy to 0 hit points.
