Shaman Attack 15Storm Guardian Spirit

Thunder crashes over your foe as the storm guardian spirit appears. This spectral creature of dark storm clouds then surrounds your ally and avenges attacks against that ally.

Daily        Implement, Primal, Thunder
Standard Action      Ranged 5

Target: One creature

Attack: Wisdom vs Reflex

Hit: 3d10 + Wisdom modifier thunder damage.

Miss: Half damage.

Effect: Choose an ally within 5 squares of the target. Until the end of the encounter, when any enemy hits that ally, that enemy takes 5 thunder damage and is pushed 1 square from the ally.


Published in Player's Handbook 2, page(s) 127.
