Cleric Attack 23Moon Tide

You slam your weapon to the ground, unleashing a blast of light that emulates the tidal forces of the moon.

Encounter        Divine, Healing, Radiant, Weapon
Standard Action      Close blast 3

Target: Each enemy in the blast

Attack: Wisdom vs. Fortitude

Hit: 3[W] + Wisdom modifier radiant damage, and you push the target up to 3 squares.

Effect: Each ally in the blast can spend a healing surge.


Published in Neverwinter Campaign Setting, page(s) 62.
