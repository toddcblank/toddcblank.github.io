Ring of FireblazingLevel 14 Uncommon

Fire springs from the hand that bears this red steel ring.

Ring Slot        21,000 gp

Property

As a standard action, ignite any unattended combustible object (such as cloth, oil, paper, tinder, or a torch) that you touch.

Power (Fire) Daily (Standard Action)

Make an attack: Close blast 3; Constitution + 4 or Charisma + 4 vs. Reflex; on a hit, the target takes 1d10 + Constitution modifier or Charisma modifier fire damage and ongoing 5 fire damage (save ends); on a miss, the target takes half damage and no ongoing damage.

If you've reached at least one milestone today, a hit deals 1d10 + Constitution modifier or Charisma modifier fire damage and ongoing 10 fire damage (save ends). On a miss, deal half damage and ongoing 5 fire damage (save ends).
