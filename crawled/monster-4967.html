Oblivion Moss Mindmaster
Large fey magical beast (plant)
Level 12 Elite Controller XP 1400

HP 250; Bloodied 125Initiative +6
AC 26, Fortitude 24, Reflex 22, Will 25Perception+12
Speed 2 (forest walk), climb 2Blindsight 20
Saving Throws +2; Action Points 1

Traits

Mind Blindness Aura 1

Any enemy that starts its turn within the aura treats the mindmaster as invisible until the start of the enemy’s next turn.

Standard Actions

Corroding Touch (acid) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +17 vs. AC

Hit: 1d8 + 5 acid damage, and ongoing 10 acid damage (save ends).

Scramble the Mind (psychic) At-Will

Attack: Ranged 10 (one creature); +15 vs. Will

Hit: 3d6 + 7 psychic damage, and the mindmaster slides the target 5 squares.

Scour the Mind (psychic) At-Will

Attack: Ranged 10 (one creature); +15 vs. Will

Hit: 3d6 + 7 psychic damage, and the mindmaster or one of its allies is invisible to the target until the end of the mindmaster’s next turn.

Absorb Memories (psychic) Recharge when no mossling duplicate remains

Attack: Ranged 5 (one enemy); +15 vs. Will

Hit: The target is dazed and cannot use its encounter or daily attack powers (save ends both).

Effect: The mindmaster creates a mossling duplicate of the target within 5 squares of the mindmaster. Choose the mossling minion that best reflects the target’s role and powers.

Minor Actions

Direct Mossling At-Will 1/round

Effect: One mossling minion within 10 squares of the mindmaster can move its speed as a free action.

Triggered Actions

Absorb Damage At-Will

Trigger: A mossling within 20 squares of the mindmaster takes damage.

Effect (Immediate Interrupt): The mindmaster takes the damage instead of the mossling.
