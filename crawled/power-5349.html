Avenger Utility 22Twin Step

You appear next to a foe, launch a devastating assault, and then disappear beyond its reach.

Encounter        Divine, Teleportation
Move Action      Personal

Effect: You teleport 8 squares. As the last action of your turn, you can teleport 8 squares as a free action.
