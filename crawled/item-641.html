Belt of FiendsLevel 15 Uncommon

This belt fills you with hellborn power and protects you from a devil's attacks.

Waist Slot        25,000 gp

Property

Gain a +2 item bonus to Athletics checks and Strength ability checks (but not Strength attacks).

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

You can use this power when you are hit by an attack. Gain a +3 power bonus to defense against that attack; you can decide to use the power after the DM tells you the attack die total.

If the attacker is a devil, then it also takes 20 radiant damage.



Published in Dragon Magazine 366, page(s) 49.
