Traits

Hyrsam's Song Aura 5

Any ally that starts its turn in the aura can make a saving throw against one effect that a save can end. Any enemy in the aura cannot take actions during Hyrsam’s turn. This aura deactivates if Hyrsam cannot take actions.

Revel Leader

If Hyrsam is not deafened, at the start of each of his turns, he can make a saving throw against one effect that a save can end. He can make a saving throw to end being slowed, immobilized, or restrained, even if the effect does not normally end on a save.
