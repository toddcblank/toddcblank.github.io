Orog Militant
Medium natural humanoid
Level 3 Soldier XP 150

Initiative +3        Senses Perception +2; low-light vision
HP 48; Bloodied 24
AC 20; Fortitude 17, Reflex 13, Will 14
Speed 5

Bastard Sword (standard, at-will) Weapon

+10 vs AC; 1d10+4 damage, and the target is marked until the end of the orog militant's next turn.

Relentless (standard; requires bastard sword, at-will) Weapon

+10 vs AC; 1d10+4 damage, and the target is pushed 1 square. the target is marked until the orog's next turn. The orog militant can shift into the vacated square as a free action.

Fierce Counterstrike (immediate reaction, when adjacent ally is first bloodied; requires bastard sword, encounter) Weapon

+10 vs AC; 2d10+4 damage, an the target is pushed 1 square. the orog militant can shift into the vacated square as a free action.

Discipline

Orogs gain a +2 bonus to saving throws against any ongoing effects when adjacent to an ally.

Alignment Evil        Languages Common, Giant
Str 19 (+5)      Dex 10 (+1)      Wis 13 (+2)
Con 16 (+4)      Int 9 (0)      Cha 11 (+1)

Equipment: bastard sword , hide armor , plate armor .
