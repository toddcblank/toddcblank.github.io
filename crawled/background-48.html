Cloistered Priest

I took my vows as a child in the grand temple at Tarsalee, and I’ve served there faithfully ever since.

Type: Campaign
Campaign Setting: Scales of War Adventure Path

You’ve grown up in the heart of religious worship, and you know the rituals and rites of your faith better than most. What event sent you out into the wider world? Under what circumstances will you return? Given your sheltered upbringing, how do you react to the strange and the dangerous?
Good Background: Human.
    Benefit: You add Religion to your class skill list, and you gain a +3 bonus to Religion checks.

Published in Dragon Magazine 366, page(s) 57.
