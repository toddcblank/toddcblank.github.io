Alluring VeilLevel 7 Uncommon

This gossamer veil conceals your features and augments your glamour.

Head Slot        2,600 gp

Power (Illusion) Encounter (Minor Action)

You assume the appearance of a member of any humanoid race of the same size category as you. Your clothing and equipment alter appearance as you desire. The illusion doesn't alter sound or texture, so a creature listening to you or touching you might detect the illusion.

Power Daily (Free Action)

Trigger: You make an attack roll using an encounter or daily attack power that has the charm or illusion keyword and miss.

Effect: Reroll the attack roll and use either result.



Published in Dark Sun Campaign Setting, page(s) 128.
