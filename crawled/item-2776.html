Symbol of FreedomLevel 7+ Uncommon

This symbol allows you or one of your allies to shrug off certain debilitating conditions.

Lvl 17+465,000 gp

Implement: Holy symbol

Enhancement Bonus: attack rolls and damage rolls

Critical: +1d6 damage per plus

Power Daily (Free Action)

Use this power when you hit with an attack using this holy symbol. You or any one ally within 10 squares of you can roll a saving throw against the any effect that includes the dominated, immobilized, restrained, or slowed condition. Add the symbol's enhancement bonus as a power bonus to the save.
