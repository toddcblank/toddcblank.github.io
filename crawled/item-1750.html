Loadstone of the PlanesLevel 8 Uncommon

This arrow-shaped piece of magnetite bound in electrum whirls in the air, directing you to a portal you're looking for.

Wondrous Item        3,400 gp

Power Daily

Special. Use this power during a short or an extended rest only. You learn the direction and rough distance to the nearest portal connected to a plane of your choice.
