Avenger Attack 27Covenant of Repentance

You demand penitence from those who have flouted the will of your god, and you harm those who refuse to repent.

Encounter        Divine, Implement, Necrotic
Standard Action      Ranged 5

Target: One creature

Effect: If the target is your oath of enmity target, you can roll twice on this attack and use either result.

Attack: Wisdom vs. Will

Hit: 3d8 + Wisdom modifier necrotic damage, and you knock the target prone. If the target is not prone at the end of its next turn, it takes 10 extra necrotic damage.


Published in Dragon Magazine 382, page(s) 44.
