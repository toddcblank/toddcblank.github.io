Dancing Thorn Style

Prerequisite: Eladrin, monk, Flurry of Blows class feature
Benefit: While wielding a longsword, you gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls using monk implement attack powers.
    As well, if you wield the longsword in two hands, add +1 to the damage dealt by the power granted by your Flurry of Blows class feature.

Published in Dragon Magazine 389.
