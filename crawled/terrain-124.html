At-Will TerrainSmall Dune

A small, slowly shifting dune is formed from loose sand that parts easily, then flows back to erase any trace of disturbance.

Standard Action

Requirement: You must be in a square of the dune.

Check: Athletics check (easy DC) to burrow into the dune.

Success: You bury yourself beneath the sand. You fall prone and have total concealment until you move or attack.

Published in Dark Sun Creature Catalog, page(s) 136.
