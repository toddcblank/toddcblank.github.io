Donjon’s Cave-In
Level - Single-Use TerrainXP -

Detect -

Initiative -

Standard Actions

Cave-In Encounter

Attack: Close blast 3 (creatures in the blast); +10 vs. Reflex

Hit: 2d8 + 5 damage, and the target is immobilized (save ends).

Miss: Half damage, and slide the target up to 2 squares to a square adjacent to the blast’s area.

Effect: The area becomes difficult terrain.

Published in Dungeon Magazine 196.
