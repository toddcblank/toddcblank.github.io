Ribbon of Limitless QuestionsLevel 13 Uncommon

When this blue, silky ribbon is unrolled, a new question appears on it in glowing gold and silver runes.

Wondrous Item        17,000 gp

Property

When you perform a divination ritual that allows you to ask a number of questions, such as Hand of Fate, Speak with Dead, or Consult Oracle, you can ask one extra question. This effect can allow you to exceed the normal maximum number of questions allowed.

Power Daily (Free Action)

Trigger: You fail an Intelligence- or Charisma-based skill check during a skill challenge.

Effect: Reroll the skill check with a +2 power bonus and use either result.



Published in Adventurer's Vault 2, page(s) 77.
