Adamantine CradleLevel 15 Uncommon

Dull black metal lines this massive nest and shield inhabitants from danger.

Wondrous Item        25,000 gp

Property

This layer of hardened metal suffices to line a partial sphere with a diameter of 4 squares. A creature inside the cradle has superior cover; against attacks from above, it has normal cover.

Power Encounter (Immediate Interrupt)

Trigger: An attack deals damage to you while you are in the cradle.

Effect: Reduce the damage you take from the attack by 10.



Published in Dragon Magazine 383, page(s) 47.
