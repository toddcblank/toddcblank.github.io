Improved Vigor

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Fighter, trained in Endurance
Benefit: You gain a +1 feat bonus to the number of temporary hit points you gain from using an invigorating power. The bonus increases to 2 at 11th level and 3 at 21st level.
