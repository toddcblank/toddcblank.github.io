Whenever an enemy reduces a devil to 0 hit points within 10 squares of Najala’s Gate, the gate loses its damage resistance until the end of that enemy’s next turn.

Invoke Fealty (charm) Recharge when first bloodied

Attack: Close burst 5, or 10 while bloodied (two or three creatures in the burst); +18 vs. Will

Hit: 2d10 + 5 psychic damage, and the target is dominated until the end of its next turn.
