Tempest Glyph
Object
Level 7 TrapXP 300

Detect Perception DC 27, Arcana DC 23

Initiative -

Immune attacks and damage

Triggered Actions

Attack (thunder) Encounter

Attack: Close burst 3 (creatures in burst); +10 vs. Reflex

Hit: 4d6 thunder damage, and the target slides up to 2 squares and falls prone.

Miss: Half damage.

Countermeasures

Disable: Thievery or Arcana DC 23 (the character must be adjacent to the glyph). Success: The trap is disabled after three successes. Failure (18 or less): The glyph is triggered.
