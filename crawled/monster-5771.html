Stone Cold Slayer

Any creature slain by Xander is petrified. As long as the creature remains petrified, it cannot be raised from the dead. Xander can end the effect by touching the petrified creature. The effect can also be ended by spilling one or more drops of Xander’s blood on the petrified creature.
