Attack: Melee 2 (one creature); +24 vs. Reflex

    Attack (No Action): Close burst 5 (enemies in the burst); +22 vs. Reflex

    Hit: 4d8 + 9 damage, and the target falls prone and cannot stand up (save ends).

    Effect: The quaking earth aura reverts to its original state and size (aura 1).

Triggered Actions
