Wind Devil (standard; sustain minor, at-will)

The earthwind ravager causes swirling wind to rise up around the target; ranged 5; +26 vs Fortitude; 4d8 damage, and the target is immobilized until the end of the ravager’s next turn. The creature can sustain the effect as a minor action, dealing 2d8 damage to the target and keeping it immobilized until the end of the ravager’s next turn.

Close burst 1; +24 vs Fortitude; 1d8+10 damage, and the target is pushed 2 squares.

Close burst 3; +24 vs Fortitude; 4d8+8 damage, and the target is stunned until the end of the earthwind ravager’s next turn.
