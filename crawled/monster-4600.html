When an ally flanks with the dusk harrier, that ally deals 1d8 extra damage with attacks against the flanked creature.

Harrier's Flank (immediate reaction, when an ally moves adjacent to a creature within 2 squares of the dusk harrier; at-will)

The dusk harrier shifts 2 squares. It must end this shift in a flanking position with the ally that triggered this power.
