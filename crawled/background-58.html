Haunted Veteran

I marched south into the jungle with Atrelius. Can we talk about something else?

Type: Campaign
Campaign Setting: Scales of War Adventure Path

You saw things in the war that no one should have to see, and the horrors of war changed your personality. What were you like before you went to war? Under what circumstances will you share what you experienced? How do you keep those memories at bay: By adventuring and other hardships, through prayer, or with a bottle?
Good Background: Fighter.
    Benefit: Once per encounter, reroll a failed saving throw against a fear effect.

Published in Dragon Magazine 366, page(s) 59.
