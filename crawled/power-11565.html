Barbarian Attack 13Shattering Reach

The long haft of your weapon allows you to strike with enough force to shatter armor and shields like brittle glass.

Encounter        Primal, Weapon
Standard Action      Melee weapon

Requirement: You must be wielding a two-handed reach weapon.

Target: One creature

Attack: Strength vs. AC

Hit: 3[W] + Strength modifier damage, and the target takes a penalty to AC equal to your Constitution modifier until the end of your next turn.


Published in Dragon Magazine 384, page(s) 39.
