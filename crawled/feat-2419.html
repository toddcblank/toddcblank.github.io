Furious Charge

Heroic Tier
Prerequisite: Half-orc, ranger, Hunter’s Quarry class feature
Benefit: When you miss your quarry with a charge attack, you can expend your furious assault racial power to reroll the charge’s attack roll. If the attack hits, you deal 1[W] extra damage to your quarry.

Published in Martial Power 2, page(s) 134.
