Foe Fending ShieldLevel 6+ Uncommon

This shield can fend off an attack against an ally while it inflames your enemy's ire.

Lvl 61,800 gp Lvl 261,125,000 gp
Lvl 1645,000 gp

Arms Slot: Any shield

Power Daily (Immediate Interrupt)

Trigger: An attack that targets AC or Reflex hits an ally adjacent to you.

Effect: The triggering attacker is marked (save ends). If the attacker is already marked by you or an ally, the attacker instead takes a -2 penalty to the attack.

Level 16: The triggering attack hits an ally within 3 squares.

Level 26: The triggering attack hits an ally within 5 squares.
