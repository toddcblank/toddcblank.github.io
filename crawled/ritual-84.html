Purge Spellplague

You extend the energy of your body and your magic outward into the land and living things around you, pulling from them the contaminated magic of the Spellplague.

Component Cost: 25,000 gp
Market Price: 150,000 gp
Key Skill: ArcanaLevel: 24
Category: Exploration
Time: 30 minutes
Duration: Instantaneous

You remove the effects of the Spellplague from the land around you. Your Arcana check result determines the radius of the area affected. That region is cleansed of the Spellplague, though there is no guarantee it will remain so.
