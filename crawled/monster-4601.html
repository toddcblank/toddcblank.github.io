Traits

Ramshackle Armor

The first time the kobold is hit during an encounter, its homemade armor falls apart, and it takes a -4 penalty to AC until the end of the encounter.

Standard Actions

Club (weapon) At-Will

Attack: Melee 1 (one creature); +8 vs. AC

Hit: 1d6 damage.

Sack of Junk At-Will

Attack: Area burst 1 within 10 (creatures in the burst); +6 vs. Reflex

Hit: 1d6 + 3 damage, and if the target is in the origin square of the burst, it takes ongoing 5 damage (save ends).

Minor Actions

Shifty At-Will

Effect: The kobold shifts 1 square.
