Pick (standard, at-will)

+6 vs AC; 4 damage, and Sorik shifts 1 square.

Roll With It (immediate interrupt, when hit by an attack; encounter)

Sorik can roll a saving throw to avoid damage from the attack. If he succeeds, he can shift 2 squares.

Stand Your Ground

When an effect pulls, pushes, or slides Sorik, Sorik moves 1 square less than the effect specifies. When an attack could knock Sorik prone, a successful save negates that effect.
