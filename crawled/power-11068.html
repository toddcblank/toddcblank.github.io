Ardent Utility 2Wellspring of Vigor

By shutting down his or her ability to feel pain, you ensure that your ally can keep going when others might falter.

Daily        Psionic
Minor Action      Melee touch

Target: You or one ally

Effect: Until the end of the encounter, whenever the target starts his or her turn and is not bloodied, he or she gains temporary hit points equal to 1 + your Constitution modifier.


Published in Player's Handbook 3, page(s) 26.
