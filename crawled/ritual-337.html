Snowstorm Summons

You draw the power of your staff of winter out and cast it into the air, bringing sudden chill and the promise of snow.

Component Cost: Focus (staff of winter)
Market Price: 4500 gp
Key Skill: Arcana or NatureLevel: 14
Category: Exploration
Time: 30 minutes
Duration: Special

You change the weather within a 2-mile radius of the staff of winter to that of a heavy blizzard in the dead of winter. If the staff moves, the effect of the ritual moves with it. The bearer of the staff controls the blizzard’s general tendencies, such as the direction and intensity of the wind or the degree of obscurity from snow. The bearer of the staff cannot control specific applications of the weather, such as specific gusts of wind or specific squares of obscuring terrain. The altered weather persists for a duration you choose up to the maximum determined by your skill check result.
