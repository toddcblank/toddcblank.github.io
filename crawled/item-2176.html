Resplendent BootsLevel 6 Uncommon

These intricately embroidered boots let you fade from sight for a moment to slip past your foes.

Feet Slot        1,800 gp

Property

You gain an item bonus to all defenses against opportunity attacks equal to the number of items you have from this set.

Power (Illusion) Daily (Move Action)

You become invisible and move your speed. You become visible again at the end of this action.
