Belt of the Flanker's BaneLevel 6 Uncommon

When wearing this belt, a subtle shift of your weight is all you need to send a flanking enemy scrambling.

Waist Slot        1,800 gp

Power Encounter (Immediate Interrupt)

Trigger: When a flanking enemy makes an attack roll against you.

Effect: You slide the triggering enemy 2 squares to a square adjacent to you.



Published in Dragon Magazine 381, page(s) 97.
